The proposed MDC Jordan    Downs is a new LEED‐Gold rated infill development consisting of 135    apartments within the Jordan Downs master planned community. The project    would improve site accessibility by extending Century Boulevard through the    existing Jordan Downs housing project, opening up the 100‐acre community to    bikes, pedestrians, and cars. In order to facilitate active transportation,    Century Boulevard would be built as a complete street, with traffic calming,    wide sidewalks, bike lanes, shade trees, and a re‐routed bus line.
