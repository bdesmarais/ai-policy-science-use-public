Gipson welcomes new class of Assemblymembers
SACRAMENTO,  Calif ---
Assemblymember  Mike A. Gipson (D-Carson) was tapped today by Assembly Speaker Anthony Rendon  (D-Paramount) as Democratic Caucus Chair following the opening of a new  legislative session of the California State Assembly.
“It  is a true honor to be named to this position to work alongside Speaker Rendon  in guiding California toward a brighter future,” stated Gipson. “I gladly  commit to furthering the priorities of the caucus and I look forward to  providing support to the incoming class of new members.”
Gipson  was first chosen by Speaker Rendon for this position in June 2016. As Caucus  Chair he will continue to play a pivotal role in moving the Democratic agenda  forward through caucus meetings and policy summits.
