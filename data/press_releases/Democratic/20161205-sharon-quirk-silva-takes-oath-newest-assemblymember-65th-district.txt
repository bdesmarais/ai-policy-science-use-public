(SACRAMENTO)
-  Sharon Quirk-Silva (D-Fullerton) was sworn in as the Assemblymember for the 65th Assembly District.
Sharon is a mother, educator, and dedicated public servant who has served her community as Mayor of Fullerton and in the state Assembly. Sharon grew up in Orange County and received her education at Fullerton public schools as well as Fullerton College, before earning her bachelor’s degree at UCLA and her Teaching Credential from CSU Fullerton and was named among their 50 leading Latina graduate.
The 65th Assembly District includes the cities of Anaheim, Buena Park, Cypress, Fullerton, La Palma, and Stanton.
The ceremony took place on the Assembly Floor.
