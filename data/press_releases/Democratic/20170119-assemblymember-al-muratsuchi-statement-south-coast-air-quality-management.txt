TORRANCE, CA – “The South Coast AQMD has heard the concerns of South Bay residents and is proposing a ban on highly toxic hydrofluoric acid in our community.  Ending the use of this deadly chemical in the refining process is one of my top priorities as your Assemblymember and I stand with the families and refinery workers who have been at the forefront of this fight to make the region safer for everyone.  I applaud the AQMD for taking this step and look forward to working with them to protect our region and make the South Bay a better place to live, work and raise a family. “
Assemblymember Al Muratsuchi proudly represents the 66
th
Assembly District and serves as Chair of the Joint Legislative Audit Committee.  In addition, he serves on the Assembly Committee’s on Appropriations, Natural Resources, Utilities and Veteran’s Affairs.
