CARSON,  Calif
--- California Members of Congress spoke loud and clear with  their voices and their action by boycotting the inauguration of Donald Trump.  Just days before, as the nation honored Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., Trump’s  vitriolic words against civil rights leader Rep. John Lewis added to his routinely  hateful diatribe.
The California congressional boycott is a refusal to accept  this hateful and divisive behavior as normal. It also sends a message to young  and old that women deserve respect not misogynistic degrading words and behavior.  California representatives also understand that a nation inclusive of  immigrants has always contributed to our country’s strength.
If this is truly a “new day,” a humble apology to the  citizens of the United States is in order and a fitting way to begin a term as  leader of a free nation.
Assemblymember  Gipson represents the
64th Assembly
District which includes Carson,  Compton, Gardena, Harbor Gateway, Lynwood, North Long Beach, Rancho Dominguez,  South L.A., Torrance, Watts/Willowbrook and Wilmington.
