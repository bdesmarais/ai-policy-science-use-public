CARSON,  Calif ---
“Just two days after millions of women across the globe marched and  rallied for women’s rights on the 44th anniversary of the historical  Supreme Court’s decision in Roe v. Wade, President Trump responded by delivering  a strong blow against them.
Today, with the sweep of a pen, he reinstituted the Mexico City Policy  prohibiting any international family planning charities from receiving federal  funding unless they formally agree to not provide abortion services. This also  includes educating a woman about the procedure and providing informational  materials.
I  stand strong with women in protecting their right to make their own reproductive  health decisions. This is a human right and fundamental to freedom.
We  must remain vocal and vigilant as even more vital services including the right  to affordable health care will soon be on his chopping block.”
Assemblymember  Gipson represents the
64th Assembly
District which includes Carson,  Compton, Gardena, Harbor Gateway, Lynwood, North Long Beach, Rancho Dominguez,  South L.A., Torrance, Watts/Willowbrook and Wilmington.
