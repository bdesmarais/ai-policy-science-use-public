(CARSON, Calif ---) Assembly Democratic Caucus Chair Mike A. Gipson releases the following statement after tonight's joint address to congress by President Donald Trump:
"Tonight's joint address to congress, yet again, underscores the extreme agenda put forth by Donald Trump and his administration. It also demonstrates his willingness repeal a healthcare system that has been working for 5 million Californians. Thanks to the Affordable Care Act, discriminating against people with pre-existing conditions and providing healthcare for the lucky are now a thing of the past. These are real lives receiving real care. As Assembly Democratic Caucus Chair, we will continue to be 'out of control' and represent all Californians."
