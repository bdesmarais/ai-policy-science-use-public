(Carson)
– Assemblymember Mike A. Gipson will join Los Angeles Philippine Consulate General Adelio Angelito S. Cruz, Carson Councilmember Elito M. Santarina and others for an
Outreach Mission
on Saturday May 6, 2017 at the Carson Community Center (801 E Carson St, Carson 90745) from 9am – 3pm.
“I am elated to be working with so many partners on this very important event,” said Asm. Gipson. “My district represents one of the most diverse in the greater Los Angeles area.  In Carson alone, nearly 20 percent of my constituents are Filipinos. This outreach mission will provide the services that members of my community so desperately need.”
Saturday’s outreach will include: passport applications, dual citizenship applications, notarization/authentication of documents, reports of marriage/birth and overseas voting registration. For more information, participants may visit the Philippine Consulate General’s website at
www.philippineconsulatela.org
or call Riezl Pe Benito at (310) 952-1743.
Other partners include: Filipino-American Chamber of Commerce – South Bay Los Angeles Area, Farmers Insurance Agent Alex Cainglet, Commissioner Joey Cinco, Carson Adult Day Health Care Center, Dreamers and Visionaries of the South Bay, Inc., and Philippine Independence Day Foundation Inc.
Mike Gipson represents the 64th Assembly District. The district includes the cities of Carson, Compton, Gardena, Harbor Gateway, Lynwood, North Long Beach, Rancho Dominguez, South Los Angeles, Torrance, Watts/Willowbrook and Wilmington.
