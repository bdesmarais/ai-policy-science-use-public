(West Covina)
– Assemblywoman Blanca E. Rubio (D-Baldwin Park) announced her staff will be hosting mobile office hours throughout the 48
th
Assembly District. During this outreach, constituents can meet with members of the Assemblywoman’s staff to obtain assistance with issues involving state agencies, including the Department of Motor Vehicles, Consumer Affairs, Employment Development Department, Social Services, Veterans Affairs and more.
“It is my goal to ensure that all residents of the 48
th
Assembly District know that my office is committed to quality constituent service and accessibility,” said Rubio.
Assemblywoman Rubio plans to hold additional mobile offices in each of the communities she represents. For additional information regarding mobile office hours, please contact the District Office at (626) 960-4457.
