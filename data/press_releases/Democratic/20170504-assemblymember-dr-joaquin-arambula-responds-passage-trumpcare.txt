(Sacramento)
- Assemblymember Dr. Joaquin Arambula (D-Fresno) made the following statement after the U.S. House of Representatives passed Trumpcare:
“I am disheartened that the House of Representatives put politics first and their constituents second.  Millions of Californians now risk losing their health care coverage and today’s action will cost the State and the Valley thousands of health care related jobs.  I am disappointed that some of the members of Congress, whose districts have some of the largest number of enrollees, voted to dismantle the ACA.”
