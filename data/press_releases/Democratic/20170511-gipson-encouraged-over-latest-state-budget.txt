SACRAMENTO, Calif --- Assemblymember and Assembly Democratic Caucus Chair Mike A. Gipson expressed optimism on today’s May Revision of the California State Budget released by Governor Jerry Brown.
“Today’s budget makes significant improvements of what was released a few months ago.  We have yet another building block in finalizing a spending plan that reflects California’s values.   I looking forward to joining my Assembly colleagues in crafting a budget that will protect California’s progressive gains while preparing for challenges we may face from Washington. “
