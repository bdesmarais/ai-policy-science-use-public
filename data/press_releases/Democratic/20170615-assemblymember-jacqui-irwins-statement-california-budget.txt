Pleased to see State budget will continue to include funding for key programs
(Sacramento, CA) –
“Today the California Legislature passed another responsible and balanced budget on-time,” said Assemblymember Irwin. “The budget builds on state priorities to increase funding for our education system from pre-school through college, invest in the working poor, and grow a strong reserve for future downturns.”
Specifically, the California Budget includes provisions that will have a profound effect on our state and region:
Adding to the state’s rainy day fund, bringing total reserves to $9.9 billion.
Continuing the higher education Middle Class Scholarship.
Full funding to help connect veterans with the benefits that they have earned.
Prioritizing our most vulnerable veterans in California veteran homes.
Enhancing the state cybersecurity networks to protect the public’s personal information.
Including a $400,000 grant to provide updates to the Oxnard DMV.
