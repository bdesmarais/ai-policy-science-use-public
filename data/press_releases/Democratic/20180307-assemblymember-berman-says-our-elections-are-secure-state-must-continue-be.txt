SACRAMENTO
–
Assemblymember Marc Berman (D-Palo Alto) released the following statement in response to the Joint Legislative Hearing of the Assembly Elections & Redistricting Committee and the Senate Elections & Constitutional Amendments Committee. Committee members, cybersecurity experts, and elections officials discussed continued efforts by foreign operatives to influence U.S. elections and additional ways to shield California’s voting systems from increasingly sophisticated attacks.
“Our voting systems were not hacked during the 2016 elections, but they are under constant threat,” said Assemblymember Berman, Chair of the Assembly Elections & Redistricting Committee. “Californians can feel confident knowing that we are being vigilant and taking proactive steps to protect our elections from interference.”
