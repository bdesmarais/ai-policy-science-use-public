Assemblymember James Ramos
(D-Highland) is seeking community input and fresh ideas from the 40th Assembly District that could be addressed with state legislation.
The new feature on his website titled “There Ought to be a Law” provides a form with input for residents to submit proposals for new laws. The deadline to submit is February 8, 2019
“Some of the best ideas we work on in the Legislature come directly from constituents like you who are seeing problems in their neighborhoods firsthand,” said
Assemblymember Ramos
. “In the Inland Empire, we face unique circumstances that require special attention. As your representative, it is my duty to fight for you. When we work together as a community, we get better policy outcomes.”
“There Ought to be a Law” link:
https://a45.asmdc.org/there-oughta-be-law
###
Assemblymember James Ramos proudly represents the 40th Assembly district which includes Highland, Loma Linda, Mentone, Rancho Cucamonga, Redlands, and San Bernardino.
