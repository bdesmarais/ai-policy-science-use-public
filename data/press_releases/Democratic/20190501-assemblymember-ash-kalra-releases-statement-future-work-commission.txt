SACRAMENTO – Assemblymember Ash Kalra (D-San Jose), Chair of the Assembly Committee on Labor and Employment, released the following statement today in response to the establishment of the Future of Work Commission:
“I commend Governor Newsom on the creation of California’s first Future of Work Commission. The establishment of this commission on International Workers’ Day is timely and will help guide our state in leveraging technology to create a more equitable workplace. As the Chair of the Assembly Committee on Labor and Employment, I am proud to support initiatives that address how new technologies can promote quality jobs and foster income equality.”
