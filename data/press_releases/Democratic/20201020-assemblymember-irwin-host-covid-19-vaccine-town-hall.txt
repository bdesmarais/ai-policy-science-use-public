Who:
Join Assemblymember Jacqui Irwin and a group of distinguished scientists and health care leaders including Ventura County Public Health Officer Dr. Robert Levin for a virtual town hall on vaccine development for the COVID-19 virus.
What:
The conversation will cover four of the most pressing topics on the designing, testing, and mass-production of a vaccine for COVID-19 to help Ventura County residents gain a greater understanding of the extensive process it takes to produce a successful vaccine. The program will cover the following topics:
I. Basics of Vaccines
II. COVID Vaccine Trials and Approval Process
III. Status and Timeline for a COVID Vaccine
IV. Prioritization of the Vaccines and Distribution
When:
Wednesday, October 28, 2020 at 6:00 PM on Facebook LIVE via Assemblymember Irwin’s Facebook page (@AssemblymemberJacquiIrwin)
How:
To RSVP in advance and submit questions, visit
bit.ly/VaccineTownHall
.
Assemblymember Jacqui Irwin represents California’s 44th Assembly District, which includes the communities of Camarillo, Moorpark, Oak Park, Oxnard, Port Hueneme, Thousand Oaks, and Westlake Village.
