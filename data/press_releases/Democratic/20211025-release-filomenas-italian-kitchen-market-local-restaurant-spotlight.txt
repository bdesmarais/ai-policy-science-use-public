COSTA MESA
–
Filomena’s Italian Kitchen & Market has been honored as the Local Restaurant Spotlight for October for Assembly District 74. This Costa Mesa-based eatery is has been ranked the #1 restaurant in Costa Mesa by Trip Advisor and is known for its traditional and delicious Italian Cuisine.
“It was such a joy to visit Filomena’s Italian Kitchen & Market and recognize this award-winning restaurant as our October
Local Restaurant Spotlight
,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Laguna Beach)
. “With a rich Italian heritage, Chef Linda shares the traditions and techniques of her grandmother’s cooking with her guests.”
On October 22, 2021, Assemblywoman Petrie-Norris presented a resolution in recognition of Filomena’s Italian Kitchen & Market and its hardworking owners and employees, including:
Linda Johnsen, Chef and Owner
Santiago Castaneda, Sous Chef
Carlos Roman Salazar, Incoming Co-Owner
Sergio Roman Salazar, Incoming Co-Owner
Alex F. Garza, Manager
