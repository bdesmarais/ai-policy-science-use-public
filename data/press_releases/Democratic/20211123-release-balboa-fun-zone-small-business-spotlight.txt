NEWPORT BEACH
–
Balboa Fun Zone has been selected as the November Small Business Spotlight for Assembly District 74. Balboa Fun Zone is one of Southern California’s oldest and last great coastal amusement areas. Built in 1936, this family-friendly attraction boasts an array of exciting activities for visitors, including arcade games, a nautical museum and Ferris wheel rides.
“I am honored to recognize Balboa Fun Zone as our November Small Business Spotlight,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Laguna Beach)
. “Balboa Fun Zone is the perfect combination of tradition and fun— making it a go-to place for locals and tourists alike.”
“It is an honor to receive the Small Business Spotlight from Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris,” said
Henry Pyle, Balboa Fun Zone
Managing Partner.
“My family and I are excited and grateful to assume stewardship of the iconic Balboa Fun Zone and look forward to working with the city and community to restore this historic 80-year-old landmark”
On November 23, 2021, Assemblywoman Petrie-Norris was joined by Newport Chamber President Steve Rosansky to present a resolution in recognition of Balboa Fun Zone.
