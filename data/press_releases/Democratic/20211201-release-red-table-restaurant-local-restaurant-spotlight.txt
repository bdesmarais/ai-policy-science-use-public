HUNTINGTON BEACH
–
Red Table Restaurant
has been honored by Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris as part of the Local Restaurant Spotlight Program.
This woman-owned Gastrobar is a favorite among the Huntington Harbor Community and is the perfect destination for an eclectic, comfortable and enjoyable dining experience.
“It was such a joy to visit Red Table Restaurant and recognize this wonderful restaurant,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris
. “Red Table Restaurant is the perfect place to come together in community and ‘Relax, Eat and Drink,’ as is their mantra!”
Assemblywoman Petrie-Norris presented a resolution in recognition of Red Table Restaurant to hard working co-owners, Sandy Bauer and Cozette Dunlap.
