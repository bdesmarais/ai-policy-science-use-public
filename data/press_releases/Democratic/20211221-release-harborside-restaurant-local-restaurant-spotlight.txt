NEWPORT BEACH – Harborside Restaurant has been honored by Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris as the Local Restaurant Spotlight for December. This local treasure features beautiful waterfront views and has an award-winning menu. Located in the historic Balboa Pavilion with spectacular views of the Newport Harbor, Harborside Restaurant is a fixture in the community.
“It was such a joy to visit Harborside Restaurant and recognize it as our December Local Restaurant Spotlight,” said Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Laguna Beach). “The great views and amazing food make Harborside Restaurant the place to be!”
Assemblywoman Petrie-Norris was joined by Newport Beach City Councilwoman Joy Brenner to present a resolution in recognition of Harborside Restaurant and its hardworking co-owners, Doug Salisbury and David Salisbury.
