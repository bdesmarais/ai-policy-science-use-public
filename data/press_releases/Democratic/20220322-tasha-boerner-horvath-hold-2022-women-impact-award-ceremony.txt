CARLSBAD, Calif.
— Assemblymember Tasha Boerner Horvath announces the honorees for the 2022 Women of Impact Awards for Assembly District 76, which will be held
Friday, March 25 at 11 a.m.
This award was created by the Assemblymember’s office to recognize North County women who stand out in their communities as innovators, leaders, and advocates.
“Our North County communities are defined by the passionate and dedicated women working every day to improve the lives of our neighbors,” said Assemblymember Tasha Boerner Horvath. “Though it’s impossible to honor every changemaker in our community the way they deserve, I’m proud to host this event praising some of North County’s most influential women and reflect on the ways their efforts make our home a better place to live.”
The recipients by category are as follows:
Impact in Business/Entrepreneurship:
Nazeli Dertsakian
Impact to Military Community:
Shannon Morell
Impact in Education:
Dr. Cheree McKean
Impact in Civic Engagement
: Beatriz Palmer
Impact in Activism & Advocacy
: Holly Herring
Impact in Volunteerism
: Kathleen O’Leary
Impact in Health
: Colette Grant, M.D.
Impact in Community Service
: Nancy Jones
Impact in Arts and Culture
: Cheryl Ehlers
Impact as a Young Leader
: Karina and Katelynn Hardee
