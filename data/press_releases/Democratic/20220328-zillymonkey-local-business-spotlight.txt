Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris has honored zillymonkey as the Local Business Spotlight for March. This gift shop offers customers affordable and fashionable items to adorn their homes and their fun and inventive selections of items make perfect gifts for friends and family alike. They got their unique and memorable name from owner and founder
Vivian Cheng
who wanted a contemporary designer store without the feeling of being intimidating, while also conveying friendliness, cordialness and fun.
“These are some of the most fun and unique home decor items at a gift shop I have ever seen,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Irvine).
“Vivian was telling me that she loves animals and stores that start with a Z. Add that to the memorable offerings on display, and you get zillymonkey!”
“Zillymonkey is one unique gift shop founded under the slogan "
Contemporary, Modern, Affordable
" with each of our products being strategically selected based on its creative design and uniqueness, yet affordable and reasonably priced,” said
Store Owner Vivian
.
