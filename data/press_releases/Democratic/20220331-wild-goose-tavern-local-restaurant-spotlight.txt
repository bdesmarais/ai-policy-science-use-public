Costa Mesa-
Wild Goose Tavern has been honored by Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris as the Restaurant Spotlight for the month of March. This staple of Costa Mesa offers customers delectable dishes and a wide array of delightful drinks with a rustic atmosphere.
“The atmosphere at this restaurant is great” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris
“Whether you come for the food or drinks, Wild Goose Tavern is a must try.”
Chef Dan Cheri said “
Wild Goose Tavern prides itself on being a leader in the restaurant industry and local community. Our patrons have supported us for 9 years, even through a pandemic and major expansion, so we strive every day to give them the best quality in service, food and charity. The community is as much a part of our family as the team is at Wild Goose.
”
