Laguna Beach-
The Cliff has been honored by Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris as the Local Restaurant Spotlight for April. The Cliff sits on a hill, overlooking the beautiful Laguna Beach Coast. The Cliff is known for its signature cocktails, oceanfront dining, live music, and the gorgeous views.
“I’m so happy to recognize The Cliff as our Local Restaurant Spotlight for April. This family-owned favorite is a staple for the Laguna Beach community and offers world-class ocean front dining with a remarkable view,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris.
Manager Kathleen Davis
said, “We offer a stunning world class oceanfront dining experience with 180
°
whitewater views. Our customers agree, best in Laguna!”
