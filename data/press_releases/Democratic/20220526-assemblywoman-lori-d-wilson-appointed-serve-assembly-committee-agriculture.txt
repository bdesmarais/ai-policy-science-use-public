SACRAMENTO, CA
– Today, Assemblywoman Lori D. Wilson announced her appointment to serve as a Member of the Assembly Committee on Agriculture. The Committee works on issues including: agricultural commissions, agricultural commodities, Department of Food and Agriculture, expositions and fairs, farmers markets, food access, food labeling, labeling of agricultural commodities, livestock and poultry, marketing orders and agreements, milk and milk products, pest management, veterinarians: agricultural issues.
“It is an honor to be appointed to serve on the Assembly Committee on Agriculture,” said Assemblywoman Lori Wilson (D-Suisun City). “Agriculture takes place on 62% of the land in Solano County, generates almost $1.3 billion each year in gross output value in the County and creates thousands of jobs in my district.”
In addition to the Committee on Agriculture, Assemblywoman Wilson also sits on the following Committees: Accountability and Administrative Review, Appropriations, Banking and Finance and Privacy and Consumer Protection. Assemblywoman Wilson also serves on the Leadership Team as the Assistant Majority Whip.
