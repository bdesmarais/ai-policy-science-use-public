(SACRAMENTO,CA) –
Assemblymember Chris Ward (D-San Diego) released the following statement in response to the Legislature passing SCA 10:
"Today, California took an important step toward preserving the right to choose in our constitution with SCA 10,"
said Assemblymember Chris Ward (D-San Diego).
"The Supreme Court is actively working to roll back rights in this country, but California will continue to be a place where access to abortion remains safe and legal. Now, it's up to voters in November."
