Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris has recognized Plants By Yours Truly and BYO as the Local Business Spotlight for June. These businesses operate in collaboration with one another to offer quality products that promote personal, mental and environmental wellbeing. Plants By Yours Truly owner, Melissa Murillo, provides a variety of services including plant care, interior plant design, and plant design for events while championing mental health awareness. In perfect complement, the owner of BYO, Julie Darrell, offers a carefully curated selection of environmentally sustainable and reusable personal care and home goods.
“Plants By Yours Truly and BYO are amazing businesses and I am so happy to recognize them as the Small Business Spotlights this month,” said Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris. “These woman-owned businesses are offering fantastic products while also promoting the values of sustainability and positive mental health.”
BYO Owner Julie Darrell said, “
BYO Huntington Beach is your local resource for making simple swaps to reduce harmful plastic waste that impact our beach communities. We encourage customers to bring their own container to refill on nontoxic cleaning and personal care products. Small steps will have a big impact on preserving our health and environment.”
