NEWPORT BEACH-
Studio C Florals has been honored by Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris as the Local Business Spotlight for August. It is a woman owned small business that started in 2014. Crystal Schachter, owner and designer creates floral designs for any occasion including bridal bouquets, weddings, and private dinner parties.
Her goal is to create an atmosphere of dreamlike, peaceful energy for her clients. Crystal takes inspiration from her hometown, Santa Barbara, to create her floral design from the sunken gardens of the hillside, the colors and tones of the architecture downtown, and the free-flowing vegetation of the Earth.
“I had the pleasure of meeting Crystal and visiting Studio C Florals,” said
Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Irvine).
“Her work is so inspiring, you can see her passion and dedication to make her clients happy.”
“My favorite part about the work I do is hearing my clients’ stories,” said Crystal Schachter. “They inspire every detail and I work hard to make each arrangement and every event a very special and memorable one.”
Shortly after opening her shop in late 2019 the pandemic hit. Crystal faced some difficulties with importing flowers, which was her main supply. So, instead of closing down, she turned to local farms and shaped her business into a popular flower shop in Newport Beach and focused on deliveries and gifts.
