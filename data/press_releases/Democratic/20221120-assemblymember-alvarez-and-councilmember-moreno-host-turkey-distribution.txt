San Diego, CA – Assemblymember David Alvarez (D-San Diego) and San Diego Councilmember Vivian Moreno (District 8) will host a turkey distribution tomorrow at the Sherman Heights Community Center for 200 families.
Along with turkeys, families will also receive side dishes for a complete Thanksgiving meal. These meals were provided by the Jacobs Cushman San Diego Food Bank and made possible with donors including California-American Water, AT&T, Cox Communications, LIUNA Local 89.
WHO: Assemblymember David Alvarez, Councilmember Vivian Moreno and volunteers
WHAT: Turkey and food distribution
WHEN: Monday, November 21 at 1PM
WHERE: Sherman Heights Community Center, 2258 Island Avenue, San Diego, 92102
