San Diego, CA – Assemblymember David Alvarez (D-San Diego) will hold a community swearing-in ceremony followed by an open house in his new district office.
Community members, business leaders, friends and family will gather to celebrate Assemblymember Alvarez’s reelection to the State Assembly. He was sworn in as the Assemblymember for the 80
th
District on December 5, 2022 in Sacramento. Alvarez previously won a special general election and assumed office on June 15, 2022.
WHO: Assemblymember David Alvarez, Chula Vista Mayor Mary Casillas Salas and Assemblymember Robert Rivas will perform ceremonial roles
WHAT: Swearing-in celebration
WHEN: Monday, December 12, 2022 at 4PM
WHERE: Chula Vista POA offices, 49 Third Avenue, Chula Vista 91910
