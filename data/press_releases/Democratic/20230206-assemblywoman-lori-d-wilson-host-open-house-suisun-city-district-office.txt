SUISUN CITY, CA
- The Office of Assemblywoman Lori D. Wilson is proud to announce the grand opening of our new district office. The event will take place on Friday March 10th from 2-6 pm and will be located at One Harbor Center, Ste. 270 Suisun City, CA 94585.
The new district office will serve as a hub for the community, providing a range of services and resources to the district's residents. The office will be staffed by a team of dedicated professionals who are committed to helping residents with a wide variety of issues and concerns. Attendees will have the opportunity to meet with staff members, learn about the services offered, and enjoy light refreshments.
"We are thrilled to open our new district office and to be able to provide even better services to the residents of our community," said Assemblymember Lori Wilson. "This office represents our commitment to providing high-quality, accessible services that help improve the lives of everyone in the district."
The new district office will provide a range of services, including but not limited to the following: Department of Motor Vehicles, Unemployment and Disability Insurance and Consumer Protection. The district office will also be a source of information for residents on issues such as community events, local news, and emergency preparedness.
The opening of the new district office is a significant milestone for the community and demonstrates the district’s commitment to providing excellent services to residents. We look forward to welcoming everyone to the grand opening ceremony and hope that residents will take advantage of the resources available at the new office.
