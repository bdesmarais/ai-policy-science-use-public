Exclusive enteral nutrition (EEN) as a means to induce remission is safer, improves growth, and encourages healing in children over steroid therapy.
Enteral nutrition, or EN, is a way to give the patient’s body what it needs to stay healthy. “Enteral” means passing through the intestines when nutrition is taken either orally or through a feeding tube. Enteral nutrition is usually taken in the form of a nutrient-rich formula
