Assemblymember David Alvarez to Announce Legislation to Build More Affordable Homes
San Diego, California – Assemblymember David Alvarez (D-80th District) is announcing legislation he has introduced,
Assembly Bill 1287
, which would expand California Density Bonus Law by creating a new "Middle Income Bonus Program."
What:    Press Conference
Who:     Assemblymember David Alvarez, Circulate San Diego, Mr. Rammy Cortez
(Developer & Builder, Rammy Urban Infill), and Community Members
When:   Friday, February 24, 2023 at 11:30AM
Where: Paradise Creek Apartments - 2120 Hoover Avenue, National City, CA 91950
This program would encourage housing developers to maximize existing incentives to construct deed-restricted affordable units, and to provide additional deed-restricted moderate-income units.
