LOS ANGELES, Calif.
(June 22, 2023) –
In response to
twenty-four charges lodged against the S&W Atlas Iron and Metal Corp. for its extensive contamination of air, soil, and water without regard to its neighbors at Jordan High School
, Assembly Democratic Caucus Chair
Mike A. Gipson (D-Carson)
released the following statement:
“These charges against Atlas are an absolutely necessary step, and although today was a welcome win for environmental regulation, more needs to be done,” said
Asm. Gipson.
“Twenty-one felonies and three misdemeanors reflect the gravity of this company’s irresponsibility: subjecting a low-income community of color in my district to environmental degradation that no child should face, then avoiding accountability for years if not decades. The long-running blight of their plant in a highly vulnerable neighborhood has negatively affected not just Jordan High but young parents, a neighborhood preschool, and countless others. It is time for Atlas to go. With these charges, I stand with the people of Los Angeles to not only stop Atlas from breaking the law; we ultimately need to determine what cost their extractive contamination practices inflected on land around Jordan and how that cost will be repaid to the people of Watts.”
