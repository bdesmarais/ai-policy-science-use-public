SACRAMENTO, CA
– Today, Assemblymember Robert Rivas was sworn in as the 71st Speaker of the California State Assembly. Assemblywoman Lori D. Wilson issued the following statement:
“My heart is filled with joy as I consider and celebrate the new season of leadership under the Honorable Assemblymember Robert Rivas,”
said Assemblywoman Lori D. Wilson (D-Suisun City)
. “A man that has demonstrated throughout his political career his empathy, integrity and kindness. A man that despite coming from meager beginnings overcame challenge after challenge to become the type of leader we see today. I look forward to working with Speaker Rivas as we address challenges facing 11th Assembly District and California as a whole.”
"As Speaker of the California State Assembly, I am excited to collaborate with Assemblymember Lori Wilson on our shared commitment to improving and expanding opportunities for underserved communities,”
said Assembly Speaker Robert Rivas (D-Salinas)
. “Assemblymember Wilson is a passionate leader for her constituents and, together, we will create positive change and build a brighter future for all California residents."
The 11th Assembly District encompasses all of Solano County along with portions of Sacramento and Contra Costa counties.
