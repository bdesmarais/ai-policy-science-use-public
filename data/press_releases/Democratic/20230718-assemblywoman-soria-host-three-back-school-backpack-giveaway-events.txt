MADERA
– Assemblywoman Soria will be hosting three Back to School Backpack Giveaway and Community Resource Fair events in Fresno, Madera, and Merced in August.
These events, in partnership with Binational of Central California, will provide 1,000 free backpacks to residents of Assembly District 27. All events will include food, fun activities, state and community resources, and free backpacks while supplies last. The Madera and Merced events will include health services, provided by Camarena Health and Golden Valley Health Center.
