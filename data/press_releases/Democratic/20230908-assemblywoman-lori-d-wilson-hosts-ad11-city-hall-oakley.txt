OAKLEY, CA
– Assemblywoman Lori D. Wilson, along with her dedicated District and Capitol staff, will be in Oakley on Tuesday, September 19th, 2023 for her Assembly District (AD) 11 at City Hall located at 3231 Main Street, Oakley, California 94561.
AD11 at City Hall serves as an invaluable opportunity for local constituents to engage directly with their elected representative. Assemblymember Lori D. Wilson will be attentively available to listen to the concerns, ideas, and feedback of her constituents.
For the residents of Assembly District 11 who are based in and around Oakley, this presents an occasion to engage in a face-to-face conversation with their representative. Each individual's insights and opinions are vital in shaping the decisions and actions taken by Assemblymember Wilson in the Assembly.
Constituents are encouraged to schedule a ten-minute one-on-one meeting with Assemblymember Lori Wilson from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Individuals can reach out to Tisha Simpson to schedule your time by emailing her at
Tisha.Simpson@asm.ca.gov
.
AD11 at City Hall not only underscores the commitment of Assemblymember Lori Wilson and her team to open and transparent governance, but it also reinforces the principle that elected officials are, first and foremost, public servants dedicated to the well-being and progress of their constituents. AD11 at City Hall exemplifies the essence of participatory democracy, emphasizing that every voice matters, and collective dialogue is the bedrock of effective representation.
