SACRAMENTO, CA —
Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel (D-Encino), Chair of the Assembly Budget Committee, released the following statement regarding the Legislative Analyst’s Office new revenue forecast:
“Difficult budgets require difficult choices. But I am confident that the Assembly can help craft a budget that preserves classroom funding, protects our most vulnerable communities, and uplifts working families and small businesses. We can and will meet this challenge in a manner that reflects our values and addresses the most pressing issues facing our state.”
