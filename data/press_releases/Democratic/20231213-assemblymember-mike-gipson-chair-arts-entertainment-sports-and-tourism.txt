LOS ANGELES, Calif. - (December 13, 2023) — In a momentous declaration today,
Assemblymember Mike A. Gipson (D-Carson)
, Chair of the Arts, Entertainment, Sports, and Tourism Committee, proudly announced that the city of Los Angeles has been selected to host Super Bowl LXI in 2027. The highly anticipated event will occur at the iconic SoFi Stadium, the Los Angeles Rams home.
“As the Chair of the Arts, Entertainment, Sports, and Tourism Committee, I am thrilled to announce that the SoFi Stadium will be the host venue for Super Bowl LXI in 2027,” said Assemblymember Mike A. Gipson (D-Carson). “With its state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology, SoFi Stadium is poised to provide an unparalleled experience for fans, athletes, and attendees, just like it did in 2022. The selection of Los Angeles and SoFi Stadium as the host for Super Bowl LXI is a testament to the collaborative efforts of our state’s local governments, private entities, and community stakeholders. The event is expected to generate significant economic impact, drive tourism, and create lasting memories for fans and residents. This decision solidifies Los Angeles as a hub for sports and entertainment and showcases the ongoing commitment to fostering a vibrant community. The Super Bowl is more than just a game, it’s a celebration of athleticism, culture, and unity, and we are honored to bring this spectacular event to our city once again.”
SoFi Stadium, known for its architectural marvel and cutting-edge amenities, is set to provide an unparalleled backdrop for Super Bowl LXI. The venue, located in Inglewood, California, boasts a seating capacity of over 70,000 and is equipped with world-class facilities, making it an ideal location for one of the most-watched sporting events globally. The announcement has generated widespread excitement, and fans can look forward to an unforgettable experience at SoFi Stadium.
