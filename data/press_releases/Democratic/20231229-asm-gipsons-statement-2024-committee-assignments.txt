Assemblymember Mike Gipson (D-Carson)
has released the following statement following the selection of committee assignments by
Assembly Speaker Robert Rivas (D-Hollister)
:
“I extend my sincere appreciation to Speaker Robert Rivas for his thoughtful selection of committee assignments this week,” said
Assemblymember Mike A. Gipson (D-Carson)
. “I intend to hit the ground running and offer my insight and passion to the Committees on Government Organization; Revenue and Taxation; Insurance; and Human Services. These appointments reflect my approach to legislating and addressing the diverse needs of my constituents. As an earlier announcement, I am honored and humbled to also serve as the Chair of the Assembly Arts, Entertainment, Sports, and Tourism Committee. I appreciate this incredible opportunity and am committed to working diligently to ensure that California's arts, entertainment, sports, and tourism sectors continue to thrive, bringing economic and cultural benefits to our communities. Thank you again to Speaker Rivas for his leadership moving into 2024 and beyond.”
