May is Older Californians Month, and this year, California is also celebrating the 50th anniversary of the trend-setting California Department of Aging.
As the Chair of the Assembly Committee on Aging and Long-Term Care I was proud to join California Department of Aging Director Susan DeMarois for a thoughtful discussion about aging in California on the Look West Podcast.
We take a closer look at the growing population of Californians over the age of 60, the challenges they face, and the ways California is working to improve their lives.
