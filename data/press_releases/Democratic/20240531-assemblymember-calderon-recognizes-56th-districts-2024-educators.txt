Sacramento, CA
– Assemblymember Lisa Calderon (D – Whittier) awards teachers from the 56th Assembly district in honor of Teacher Appreciation Month. Seven teachers were selected as the recipients of the inaugural 2024 Educator of Distinction Award for their dedication to advancing excellence in education.
“Our communities’ educators have demonstrated a profound commitment to caring for students who are still adjusting to life after the pandemic,” said
Assemblymember Calderon
. “One great teacher can completely change a child’s trajectory. This award recognizes the exemplary teachers in my district for their invaluable contributions to student success.”
This year’s Educator of Distinction Awardees include:
Ms. Sonia Ayala
– South El Monte High School, El Monte Union High School District
Mrs. Michelle D. Martinez
– Durfee Elementary School, El Rancho Unified School District
Ms. Michelle Esquibel-Sanchez
– Los Nietos Middle School, Los Nietos School District
Mrs. Christine Unger
– Pantera Elementary School, Pomona Unified School District
Ms. Monica Medina-Jimenez
– Armstrong Elementary School, Pomona Unified School District
Ms. Thelma Krikorian
– Whittier High School, Whittier Union High School District
Mr. Marino Angulo
– La Serna High School, Whittier Union High School District
