SACRAMENTO, CA
- Today, Assembly Speaker Robert Rivas (D-Salinas) announced the Petroleum and Gasoline Supply Committee and Assembly’s special-session schedules:
Committee Hearings
Wednesday, September 18, afternoon: informational hearing
Thursday, September 19, morning: informational hearing
Thursday, September 26, morning: bill hearing
Floor Session
Tuesday, October 1, floor session at 1 p.m.
***
Last week, Speaker Rivas
announced
the bipartisan group of 19 Assemblymembers who will serve on the Petroleum and Gasoline Supply Committee, which will be chaired by Assemblymember Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Irvine).
The Assembly also
announced
the introduction of
Assembly Bill x2-1
, co-authored by Assemblymember Cecilia Aguiar-Curry (D-Winters) and Assemblymember Gregg Hart (D-Santa Barbara), which would increase crude oil and fuel capacity, with the ultimate goal of lessening the spikes in gas prices Californians pay at the pump. Principal co-authors of AB x2-1 include Assembly members Dawn Addis (D-Morro Bay), Steve Bennett (D-Oxnard), Isaac Bryan (D-Los Angeles), Corey Jackson (D-Riverside), Ash Kalra (D-San Jose), Alex Lee (D-San Jose), and Jim Wood (D-Healdsburg).
