SACRAMENTO, CA – Today, Assemblymember Damon Connolly (D-San Rafael) was appointed as chair of the Assembly Environmental Safety and Toxic Materials Committee by Assembly Speaker Robert Rivas. The Environmental Safety and Toxic Materials Committee presides over legislation related to toxic substances and hazardous materials, hazardous waste regulation, drinking water safety, and pesticides.
“I am honored that Speaker Rivas has entrusted me with chairmanship of the Environmental Safety and Toxic Materials Committee, and the opportunity to take leadership on critical issues that directly impact communities in the North Bay and across the state,” said Assemblymember Connolly. “I look forward to collaborating with my fellow committee members to address statewide challenges, ensure accountability, and drive positive progress.”
Serving on the Assembly Committee on Environmental Safety and Toxic Materials for the 2023-24 legislative session, Assemblymember Connolly is well-versed on legislation related to protecting our environment and ensuring the health and safety of our communities. Assemblymember Connolly has a long-standing commitment to environmental stewardship, and is excited to continue to advance policies that ensure a safer, more sustainable future. The committee’s work is critical in addressing the challenges posed by climate change, pollution, and the safety of consumer products, and Assemblymember Connolly is honored and excited to take on this leadership role.
