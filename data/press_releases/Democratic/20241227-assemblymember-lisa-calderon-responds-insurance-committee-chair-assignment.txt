SACRAMENTO, CA
– Today, Assemblymember Calderon (D-Whittier) released the following statement following the announcement of her appointment as Chair of the Assembly Committee on Insurance:
“It is an honor to continue serving as Chair of the Assembly Insurance Committee. I'm grateful to Speaker Rivas for placing his trust in me.
I look forward to continuing to work with Speaker Rivas so we can advance the work that we have set out to accomplish — ensuring Californians have access to insurance.”
