Sacramento, CA
— Today, Assemblymember Robert Garcia (D-Rancho Cucamonga) was appointed to the influential Assistant Majority Leader position.
“I am incredibly honored that Speaker Rivas has appointed me to the Assistant Majority Leader position. This leadership role is instrumental in ensuring our priorities are effectively communicated and implemented throughout the legislative process. I am committed to using this position to help strengthen our efforts to tackle key issues such as education, healthcare, addressing homelessness, and increasing housing construction. I look forward to working closely with my colleagues and Majority Leader Aguiar-Curry to ensure a productive and efficient legislative session,” said Assemblymember Robert Garcia.
