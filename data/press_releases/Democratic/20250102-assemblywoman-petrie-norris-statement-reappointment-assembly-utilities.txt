Sacramento, CA
- Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris (D-Irvine) issued the statement below following her reappointment as Chair of the Assembly Utilities & Energy Committee by Speaker Robert Rivas.
"I am honored to continue serving as Chair of the Assembly Utilities & Energy Committee.
This is a pivotal moment for California, as we work to build a reliable, sustainable and affordable clean energy future. In this role, I will continue my work to lower costs and accelerate the development of essential infrastructure.
I am grateful to Speaker Rivas for his continued trust and support, and look forward to our work together to advance California's clean energy goals and deliver savings for California families."
