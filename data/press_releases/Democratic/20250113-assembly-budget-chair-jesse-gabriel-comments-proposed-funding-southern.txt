SACRAMENTO, CA
— Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel (D-Encino), Chair of the Assembly Budget Committee, released the following statement regarding proposed state funding for wildfire response and recovery in Southern California:
“We appreciate the Governor's commitment to providing funding, resources, and support to help rebuild our Southern California communities. This is deeply personal for our LA members and the communities we represent. The Assembly looks forward to working closely with our state, federal, and local partners to ensure that we are supporting impacted communities every step of the way. As we navigate extremely dangerous conditions this week, we remain profoundly grateful to the firefighters and first responders who are protecting our homes and families.”
###
Jesse Gabriel proudly represents the San Fernando Valley in the California State Legislature.
