SACRAMENTO— On Friday morning, Speaker Rivas released the following statement:
“The Moss Landing battery plant fire is a disaster and I expect accountability and answers from the company. My team is in close communication with our heroic emergency responders, who have been working around the clock to contain the fire and ensure residents are safe. Residents should follow all official safety guidelines and evacuation orders, and our team will be here to support individuals and families in the days and weeks to come.”
