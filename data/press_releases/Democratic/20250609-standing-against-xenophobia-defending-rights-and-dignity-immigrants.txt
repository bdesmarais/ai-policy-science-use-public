The ongoing and abhorrent attacks against the immigrant community in my district and throughout the State of California are not only a moral failure but an attack on the very backbone of our country. Immigrants are the heart of our workforce, building our cities, growing our food, attending our schools, and driving progress across every corner of our economy. We cannot fall for the fear driven rhetoric that this administration pushes, and we must acknowledge the dangers of following such a narrative. I must be clear that the intent of this rhetoric is to manipulate public opinion and create division through chaos. This is inhumane and unnecessary, and we must firmly reject xenophobia. It is our responsibility to stand in unwavering solidarity to defend their rights, safeguard their families and uphold their dignity, not only as workers but as vital members of society.
I’m holding a select committee hearing as the chair of the Select Committee on Racism, Hate, and Xenophobia in Sacramento this month to do my part on calling out Trump's dark attacks on immigration. Please stay tuned for more information.
