SACRAMENTO - California Attorney General Rob Bonta has signed on in support of my Senate Bill 605 that would ensure competitive compensation for state attorney and administrative law judges by aligning their salaries with those of public sector attorneys across California.

In his support letter, the Attorney General Bonta wrote: “California Attorneys, Administrative Law Judges (CASE) members play a critical role in providing justice for all Californians as they uphold the state’s legal and administrative systems.”
