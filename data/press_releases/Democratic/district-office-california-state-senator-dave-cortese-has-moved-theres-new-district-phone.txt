SANTA CLARA COUNTY – California State Senator Dave Cortese (D-Silicon Valley) has moved the location of his Senate District 15 office from Campbell to San Jose. As a result of the population changes highlighted by the 2020 Census, the boundaries for the Congressional, State Senate, State Assembly, and Board of Equalization districts were modified. The California Citizens Redistricting Commission is responsible for eliciting public input and determining the new boundaries.
