Senator Becker Invites Folks To Talk Over Coffee At

Mazra Mediterranean Restaurant in San Bruno On February 16

(Palo Alto) – Concerned about our environment and want to talk with one of the Legislature’s leading voices on climate change?

Having a problem with the Department of Motor Vehicles or another state agency and need a little help?

Want to hear from your State Senator about the legislation he’ll be working on in 2023?

Anyone who wants to talk about these issues or any other state-related matter is invited to stop by Mazra Mediterranean Restaurant in San Bruno from 9:30am to 10:30am this Thursday, February 16, for another “Java With Josh,” featuring Senator Josh Becker (D-Menlo Park) and special guest San Bruno Mayor Rico Medina.

“I really enjoy talking directly with folks to hear what’s on their minds,” said Becker.  “These are very informal gatherings where I talk a little bit about what I’m working on, but they’re really designed to give district residents the chance to share their thoughts with me.

“Some people love to talk policy, while others just need help getting through to a state agency,” continued Becker.  “I’ll have some members of my staff with me, so in cases where we can’t help people right on the spot, they’ll certainly have a personal contact who they can follow up with later on.”

First elected in 2020, Senator Becker represents the 13th Senate District covering portions of Santa Clara and San Mateo counties and includes the cities of Atherton, Belmont, Half Moon Bay, Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, Menlo Park, Millbrae, Mountain View, Pacifica, Palo Alto, Redwood City, San Bruno, San Carlos, San Mateo, South San Francisco, and Woodside.
