***IMPORTANT UPDATE-2020 CENSUS DEADLINE***

The federal government has moved the deadline to complete and return the 2020 Census to Sept. 30, 2020.

While you and your families are staying safer at home, please complete the 2020 Census online, by phone or return the paper form mailed to your home earlier this year.

It takes only minutes complete, and the information you provide stays confidential.

Completing the 2020 Census will help ensure District 32 receives its fair share of federal funding for housing, jobs, better roads, schools, public safety and programs benefiting seniors, children, families, and the entire community, as well as other services

An accurate count also means California is fairly represented in the United States Congress.

The 2020 Census will provide a snapshot of our nation-who we are, where we live, and so much more.

The results of this once-a-decade count determine the number of seats each state has in the House of Representatives. They are also used to draw congressional and state legislative districts.

School lunches. Plans for highways. Support for firefighters and families in need. Census results affect your community every day.

Fill out the Census today.

You can mail your completed paper form, call 844-330-2020, or visit https://mycensu2020.gov/ online to be counted today.
