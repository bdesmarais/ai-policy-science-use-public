SACRAMENTO – State Senator Dave Cortese (D-Silicon Valley) signed this letter from California’s Senate and Assembly members to the California Republican Congressional Delegation urging them to request that the President end the crackdowns on hardworking, taxpaying immigrants in Southern California and throughout the state, as the actions are causing significant harm to our economy.
