WHAT: California State Senate Redistricting Hearing; Senate Elections and Constitutional Amendments Committee

WHEN: 9:30 a.m. August 19, 2025

WHO: Senate Elections and Constitutional Amendments Committee Chair – Senator Sabrina Cervantes

Sabrina Cervantes is a working mother of triplets and wife who proudly represents the 31 st District of the California State Senate, which encompasses the Inland Empire cities of Corona, Eastvale, Fontana, Jurupa Valley, Menifee, Moreno Valley, Perris, Riverside, and San Jacinto, and the unincorporated communities of Coronita, El Cerrito, Good Hope, Highgrove, Home Gardens, Lakeview, Meadowbrook, Mead Valley, Nuevo, and Romoland.
