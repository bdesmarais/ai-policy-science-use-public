Annual day of action for basic income taking place in 30+ cities around the world

On September 25, 2021, as part of the third annual Basic Income March, Senator Cortese will join a Mountain View’ event that will be reflective of the amazing progress made in the fight for basic income over the past year. The march will bring together artists, elected officials, local organizers, community organizations, basic income thought leaders and more to commemorate the growth of the movement, highlight important new policies and efforts, and energize the local grassroots community around achieving a national basic income. A full list of national and international marches can be found here .

Mountain View’s rally will partner with Gerald Huff Fund for Humanity and Level Up California to gather supporters and advocates of Universal Basic Income at the Courtyard at 500 Civic Plaza for a fun festival that will feature the  following speakers:
