Senator Anna M. Caballero Statement on Chair Appointment to Appropriations Committee

Sacramento- Senator Anna M. Caballero (D-Merced) released the following statement on her appointment as the Chair of the Senate Appropriations Committee:

“I am entering the 2024 legislative cycle energized and excited for the work ahead. I am thankful for the trust placed in me by Pro Tem McGuire, and feel honored to serve alongside such a diverse group of colleagues. During my time in the Senate representing a largely rural, agricultural area, I have worked to shine a light on the needs of working families. Good paying jobs, affordable housing and a quality education are part of every person’s California Dream. As the Chair of the Appropriations Committee it is my duty to evaluate hundreds of Senate and Assembly bills to ensure that California is investing resources where they are needed most.”

For interviews or inquiries, please contact: Elisa Rivera at (559) 264-3070

Senator Anna M. Caballero represents the 14 th Senate District, composed of majority portions of Merced, Fresno and Madera Counties.

* Senator Anna M. Caballero serves as the Interim Representative for the Salinas Valley and San Benito County until 2024 when a newly elected Senator will assume the district
