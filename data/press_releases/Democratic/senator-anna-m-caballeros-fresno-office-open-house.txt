Senator Anna M. Caballero’s Fresno Office Open House

Fresno- Senator Anna M. Caballero (D-Merced) will host an open house at her new Fresno District office and invites constituents to join. The event is an opportunity to see the new office, connect with staff and hear about the Senator’s legislative goals for 2024. Light refreshments and appetizers provided.

Fresno District Office Open House

WHEN: Friday, January 19, 2024 3:00-5:00 PM

WHERE: 2550 Mariposa Mall Fresno, CA 93721

Honored Guest:

Juan Felipe Herrera, Fowler native who served as our nation’s 21 st Poet Laureate. During the last three decades, Professor Herrera has received numerous awards and fellowships and authored thirty books, including collections of poetry, prose, short stories, young adult novels and picture books for children.

Presentation of Resolutions:

Armando Valdez, (Director and Founder), Community Center for the Arts and Technology or CCAT 25 th Anniversary Resolution

Hugo Morales, Executive Director, Radio Bilingüe, Bestowed Harvard Honorary Doctorate

Gloria Hernandez, Community Organizer, Lifetime Achievement Award

Featured Art:

Juan Arambula, Photography of Central Valley Agricultural Landscapes

Chris Schneider, Photography Featuring Fresno County Cultural Events

Student Art Provided by Michele Mazzei, Arts Department Chair at Edison High School

Prints Provided by Fresno City College

*For more information, see attached flyer.

To RSVP, please call the Senator’s Fresno District office (559) 264-3070 or email David Santos at David.Santos@Sen.Ca.Gov

This event gives community members the opportunity to meet their Senator and meet the fantastic staff that field their inquiries and help with state issues. The Senator will recognize local organizations and individuals who have made significant contributions to improving the lives of people and communities of California's 14th Senate District.

For interviews or inquiries, please contact: Elisa Rivera at (559) 264-3070

Senator Anna M. Caballero represents the 14 th Senate District, composed of majority portions of Merced, Fresno and Madera Counties.

* Senator Anna M. Caballero serves as the Interim Representative for the Salinas Valley and San Benito County until 2024 when a newly elected Senator will assume the district
