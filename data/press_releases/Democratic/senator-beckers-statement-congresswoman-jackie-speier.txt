MENLO PARK – “Congresswoman Jackie Speier is a true superhero. Throughout her incredible career in public service, she has done so much for the people of the Peninsula, our state and our country,” said state Senator Josh Becker.

“We all owe her an enormous debt of gratitude. Her courage and her forthright representation have been an inspiration for me and countless others.

“That should be our focus today: honoring Jackie for all she has accomplished. Her legacy is tremendous at every level of government.”
