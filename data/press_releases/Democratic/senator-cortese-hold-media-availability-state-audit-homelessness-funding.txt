WHAT: State Senator Dave Cortese (D-San Jose) will be available to speak to news media following the release of an audit he requested from the California State Auditor on state and local homelessness funding. The audit is expected to be released the morning of Tuesday, April 9.

WHEN: Tuesday, April 9, from 11:15 a.m. to 11:45 a.m. Senator Cortese will be available in person, by phone, or via video conferencing. Please contact Allen Young in advance at 916-216-5803.

BACKGROUND: California has invested over $20 billion since 2019 to address the homelessness crisis. In March 2023, the Joint Legislative Audit Committee (JLAC) approved an audit request by JLAC member Senator Cortese related to homelessness funding throughout the state, as well as in the City of San Jose and an additional California city selected by the State Auditor. Senator Cortese initiated the audit in 2022 following a tour of the Columbus Park encampment in San Jose, which at the time was one of the largest homeless encampments in California. It received bipartisan support.
