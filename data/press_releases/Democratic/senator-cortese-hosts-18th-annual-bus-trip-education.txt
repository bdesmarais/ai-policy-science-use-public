WHAT: Today, May 20 th , at 6:00pm, Senator Cortese hosts his 18th annual Sacramento Bus Trip for Education - this year, the Bus Trip will be virtual!

For almost two decades, Senator Cortese has hosted yearly trips to Sacramento where hundreds of students, parents, educators, and community leaders have met directly with State Officials and Legislators to advocate for adequate funding for education, ask questions, voice their concerns, and learn about key public education legislative proposals as well as the state’s recommended budget before it is signed into law.

Join this year’s virtual "Bus Trip" to engage in conversations about current legislative and funding proposals for K-12 schools, community colleges and universities. Visiting the Bus Trip this year will be Senators John Laird, Josh Becker, Steve Glazer, Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh and Bob Wieckowski, Assemblymembers Ash Kalra and Alex Lee as well as more special guests!

WHEN: Thursday, May 20, 2021 at 6:00pm PDT

RSVP: www.tinyurl.com/SD15BusTrip .

LIVESTREAM: The event will be hosted over ZOOM and livestreamed with closed captioning options available on the Senate District 15 website and Facebook Page .
