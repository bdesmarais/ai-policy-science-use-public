MENLO PARK, CA – Senator Josh Becker (D-Menlo Park) is proud to announce the winners of the 2025 Senate District 13 Peninsula Proud Photography Contest , celebrating the talent and vision of local photographers who have captured the beauty, diversity, and spirit of the Bay Area Peninsula.

This year’s winners are:

Eric Corpuz, Foster City – "Christmas Morning - Dawn in Foster City"

George Bordi, La Honda – "Teamwork: A challenging emergency medical rescue deep in the redwoods near Portola State Park succeeded thanks to the La Honda Fire Brigade, CAL FIRE Skylonda, American Medical Response, Stanford Life Flight, and San Mateo County Sheriff working together."

Matt McWright, Atherton – "Day Breaks On the Colony"

“These breathtaking photographs showcase the Peninsula’s natural beauty, rich community spirit, and resilience,” said Senator Becker . “I am thrilled to display these winning images in my Capitol Office in Sacramento and feature them on my website for all to see. Congratulations to our talented winners, and thank you to everyone who participated in this year’s contest. Your creativity highlights what makes our home so special.”

The Peninsula Proud Photography Contest is an annual celebration of local artistry and the vibrant landscapes and communities of Senate District 13.

For more information and to view the winning photographs, visit Senator Becker’s website .
