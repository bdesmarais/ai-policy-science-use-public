SACRAMENTO, CA – Senator Josh Becker (D-Menlo Park) has been appointed by Senate President pro Tempore Mike McGuire to serve as Chair of the Senate Committee on Energy, Utilities, and Communications. This appointment places Senator Becker at the forefront of shaping California’s energy policies, with wide-ranging implications for the state’s economy, workforce, and global leadership in clean energy.

“I am honored and deeply grateful to Senate President pro Tempore Mike McGuire for this opportunity,” said Senator Becker . “At a time when energy policy will determine not only California’s environmental future but also its economic resilience, I am committed to advancing clean energy, affordability, and balanced, effective regulations. Leading this committee is a responsibility I take very seriously, and I look forward to collaborating with all stakeholders to drive meaningful progress.”

As Chair, Senator Becker will prioritize initiatives aimed at lowering energy costs for Californians, expanding clean energy production, and ensuring regulations foster a resilient, equitable energy system. With more than two decades of experience championing clean energy solutions, Senator Becker is recognized as a dedicated leader in California’s ongoing efforts to combat climate change.

Despite California’s global reputation for climate leadership, the state faces significant challenges in meeting key climate targets, including reducing greenhouse gas emissions by 40% below 1990 levels by 2030. Senator Becker intends to work closely with Governor Gavin Newsom, legislative colleagues, and industry leaders to accelerate efforts toward achieving these critical goals.

“California’s leadership in clean energy and innovation is undeniable, but we must do more to build a future where every resident benefits from our progress,” Senator Becker added. “By focusing on affordability, fostering innovation, and promoting equity, we can create an energy future that is not only cleaner and more reliable but also inclusive of all communities.”

Senator Becker’s appointment signals a renewed focus on advancing forward-thinking energy policies that safeguard California’s economic competitiveness while ensuring environmental sustainability for future generations.
