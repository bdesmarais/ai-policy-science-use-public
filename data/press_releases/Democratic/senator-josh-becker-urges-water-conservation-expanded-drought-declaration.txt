For Immediate Release – Office of State Senator Josh Becker – Thursday, July 8, 2021

Senator Josh Becker Urges Water Conservation with Expanded Drought Declaration

SACRAMENTO – Senator Josh Becker, D-Peninsula, shared this statement today following the Governor’s expanded emergency drought declaration:

“Water is a precious essential resource. With San Mateo and Santa Clara counties now included in the Governor’s emergency drought proclamation, we all must take steps to ensure we are conserving water and doing our utmost to further water resilience,” Senator Becker said.

“This spring, I held a town hall with water experts on resiliency innovations as well as actions everyone can take now in the face of this emergency. For more information and water-saving tips, visit the California Department of Water Resources and SaveOurWater.com .”
