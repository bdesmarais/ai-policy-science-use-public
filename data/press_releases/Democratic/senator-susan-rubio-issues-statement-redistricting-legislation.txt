SACRAMENTO, Calif. – Senator Susan Rubio (D-Baldwin Park) issued the following statement on redistricting legislation:

“I was proud to stand alongside my colleagues in the Senate and Assembly and with Governor Gavin Newsom as we signed historic legislation that puts the power back in the hands of Californians.

At a time when our communities are under attack from unlawful raids, unjust federal cuts, and nationwide efforts  to silence voters, California is standing strong.

These powerful measures will ensure that redistricting of our congressional seats will be decided by the people, not by politicians or partisan interests.

We are fighting back and we are protecting what matters most.”

Senator Rubio spent 20 years as a classroom teacher before being elected to the California Senate in 2018.

For more information, visit the website of Senator Susan Rubio at senate.ca.gov/rubio
