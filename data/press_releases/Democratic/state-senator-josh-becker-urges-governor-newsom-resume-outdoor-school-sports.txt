For Immediate Release

MENLO PARK – State Senator Josh Becker, D-Peninsula, wrote Governor Gavin Newsom today to ask for the immediate resumption of outdoor and low-contact indoor high school sports in California. Senator Becker wants outdoor and low-contact high school sports to resume now provided that other state-recommended safety protocols are in place. The letter is linked here .
