"My heart is broken by the devastating loss of three courageous deputies in East Los Angeles. They weren’t just uniforms – they were our neighbors, our friends, and members of the LASD family who dedicated their lives to keeping others safe. Today, we mourn alongside their loved ones and stand with every deputy, officer, and first responder who risks their life in service to our communities. We will carry their memory with us and will stand with those who wear the badge."

Senator Susan Rubio D-Baldwin Park
