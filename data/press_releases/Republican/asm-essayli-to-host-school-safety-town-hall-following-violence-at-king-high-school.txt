RIVERSIDE, Calif., May 12, 2023 – Assemblyman Bill Essayli will host a school safety town hall following violence at King High School in his district. WHAT: Assemblyman Bill Essayli hosts public town hall to hear school safety concerns from parents and discuss policy solutions with the legislator, law enforcement officials and an elected school board member. WHEN: Tuesday, May 16, 2023 at 6:00 PM WHERE: Woodcrest Library 16625 Krameria Ave, Riverside, CA 92504 WHO:

Assemblyman Bill Essayli

Sheriff Chad Bianco

Elected School Board Member

Riverside Police Department Student Resource Officer Sergeant

WHY: In April, a fight at King High broke out following a transgender student repeatedly using the female locker room. Amid this and other recent acts of violence at Riverside USD Schools , Assemblyman Essayli invited Riverside School Board Members to a public town hall to discuss policy solutions to ensure the safety of our children on campus. The Riverside USD ignored the townhall invitation and instead offered a private meeting later in the summer . Assemblyman Essayli insisted parents have a right to hear from their elected officials now, in a public setting to discuss the urgent topic of school safety and announced this town hall , emphasizing any Riverside USD Board Members and administrators are welcome and encouraged to attend.
