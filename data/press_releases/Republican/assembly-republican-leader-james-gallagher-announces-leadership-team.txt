SACRAMENTO – Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (Yuba City) announced the Assembly Republican leadership team for the 2023-24 legislative session.
“I’m proud to announce my leadership team for the coming year.  Our Caucus made great strides this past year, significantly improving our communications operation, strengthening relationships with key stakeholder groups, and winning key legislative races.” said Gallagher. “We have a solid foundation and a united group of leaders who are inspired to take us to the next level.  I’m really excited for what the future holds and looking forward to maximizing our potential as a Caucus to deliver results for California families.”
The following individuals were named to top leadership positions:
Caucus Chair: Tom Lackey (R-Palmdale)
Caucus Elections Chair: Vince Fong (R-Central Valley)
Caucus Operations Chair: Devon Mathis (R-Porterville)
Caucus Communications Chair: Megan Dahle (R-Bieber)
Floor Leader: Heath Flora (R-Ripon)
Chief Whip: Laurie Davies (R- Laguna Niguel)
Deputy Floor Leader: Bill Essayli (R-Corona)
Deputy Whip: Joe Patterson (R-Rocklin)
Deputy Whip: Diane Dixon (R-Newport Beach)
Deputy Whip: Kate Sanchez (R- Rancho Santa Margarita)
