SACRAMENTO – Today,
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher
(Yuba City) announced the Assembly Republican leadership team for 2024.
“I’m proud to announce my leadership team for the coming year. Our Caucus made great strides in 2023, improving our communications operation, strengthening relationships with constituent groups and scoring major legislative victories in the fight against fentanyl, increased penalties for human traffickers and holding the line on tax increases,”
said
Gallagher
“We have a solid foundation and a united group of leaders inspired to take us to the next level. I’m excited to build on our momentum from last year and look forward to maximizing our potential as a Caucus to deliver results for California families.”
The 2024 Leadership Team includes:
Caucus Chair:
Tom Lackey
(R-Palmdale)
Floor Leader:
Heath Flora
(R-Ripon)
Deputy Floor Leader:
Kate Sanchez
(R-Rancho Santa Margarita)
Caucus Policy Chair:
Joe Patterson
(R-Rocklin)
Caucus Communications Co-Chair:
Diane Dixon
(R-Newport Beach)
Caucus Communications Co-Chair:
Laurie Davies
(R-Laguna Niguel)
Chief Whip:
Juan Alanis
(R-Modesto)
