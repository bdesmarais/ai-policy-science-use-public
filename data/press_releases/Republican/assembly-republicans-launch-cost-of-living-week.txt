Democrats stalled. Republicans are taking the lead on real solutions.
SACRAMENTO — Assembly Republicans today launched
Cost of Living Week
to expose the failed policies driving up prices across California and to demand action that delivers real relief for working families.
Legislative Democrats
promised to fix
the state’s affordability crisis, then
admitted they had no plan
. While families paid more and
fled the state
, they blocked real solutions.
Now Assembly Republicans are turning up the pressure and demanding action.
“We don’t need another
‘select committee’
to tell us what most Californians already know,”
said Assembly Republican Leader J
ames Gallagher
(East Nicolaus).
“Democrats admit that the cost of living is the top threat to our future. So why do they keep pushing policies that raise prices, expand bureaucracy, and hammer working families with higher taxes? Republicans are done waiting. Californians need relief now. If Democrats are serious, they’ll stop blocking solutions and start working with us. Let them explain why they keep standing in the way.”
Assembly Republicans are pushing solutions that include:
Lowering gas prices
by suspending costly mandates and hidden taxes
Making housing more affordable
by cutting red tape and streamlining construction
Delivering insurance rate relief
and restoring a functioning market for homeowners
Enacting a middle-class tax cut
to help families keep more of what they earn
Reducing utility rates
by reforming energy policies that punish consumers
Earlier this year, Assembly Republicans
introduced
a legislative package to reduce costs, but
Democrat leadership blocked many of these bills
, some without even granting a hearing.
“Assembly Republicans are standing up for working Californians with real solutions, not more excuses,”
Gallagher
said.
