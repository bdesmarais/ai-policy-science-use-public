SACRAMENTO
— After weeks of public outcry and sustained pressure from Assembly Republicans,
Democratic leaders announced
they will reverse course on AB 379 and restore automatic felony charges for adults who solicit sex from 16- and 17-year-olds.
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher issued the following statement:
“This is another big win for common sense, brought to you by Assembly Republicans. I want to thank the people of California for helping us stand up to the radical Left and deliver another victory for public safety. I also applaud Assemblymember Maggy Krell for her courageous stand and her willingness to push the envelope within her caucus.”
Assembly Republicans forced the majority party to backpedal after Democrats initially blocked tougher penalties for those who purchase or solicit sex. The change comes after Republicans rallied public support to protect exploited minors.
Assembly Republicans will continue pushing for policies that put the safety of children first and ensure predators face real consequences.
