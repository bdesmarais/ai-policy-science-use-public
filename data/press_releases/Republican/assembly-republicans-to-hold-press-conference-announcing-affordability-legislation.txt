SACRAMENTO – On
Wednesday, February 26
, at
8:30 a.m.
, Assembly Republicans will hold a press conference to unveil a comprehensive legislative package aimed at tackling California’s affordability crisis.
Decades of Democratic leadership have made California unaffordable for working families. Skyrocketing costs for food, gas, housing, insurance, and utilities—combined with some of the highest taxes in the nation—have forced countless families and businesses to leave the state.
Assembly Republicans are committed to providing relief by introducing a series of bills designed to lower costs and ease the financial burden on Californians.
