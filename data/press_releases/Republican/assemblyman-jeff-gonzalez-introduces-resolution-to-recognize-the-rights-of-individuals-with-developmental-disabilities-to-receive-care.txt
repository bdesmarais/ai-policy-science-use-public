SACRAMENTO – Today, Assemblyman Jeff Gonzalez (R-Indio) issued the following statement on ACR 55, which recognizes the 40th anniversary of the California Supreme Court decision that upheld the Lanterman Act entitlement:

“As a father of a son with developmental disabilities, the significance of the ARC vs. DDS decision is deeply personal to me. That is why I am proud to author ACR 55 and grateful for the co-authorship of Assemblymember Nguyen, and Senators Niello and Valladares.

In many other states, individuals with developmental disabilities and their families face wait times of over a decade to receive the support and services necessary to live integrated, fulfilling lives within their communities. California has long been a leader in safeguarding essential services for individuals with disabilities, and I take great pride in continuing that legacy.”
