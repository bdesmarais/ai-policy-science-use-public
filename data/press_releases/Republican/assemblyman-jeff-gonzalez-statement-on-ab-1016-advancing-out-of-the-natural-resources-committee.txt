SACRAMENTO – Today, Assemblyman Jeff Gonzalez (R-Indio) issued the following statement on AB 1016 successfully passing through the Assembly Natural Resources Committee:

“The advancement of AB 1016 out of committee is a significant step forward in harnessing California’s untapped geothermal energy. This bill not only paves the way for clean energy innovation but also provides communities like Imperial County with the tools they need to lead in this growing sector. By investing in geothermal energy, we can create sustainable, well-paying jobs that will benefit our region for years to come,” said Gonzalez.
