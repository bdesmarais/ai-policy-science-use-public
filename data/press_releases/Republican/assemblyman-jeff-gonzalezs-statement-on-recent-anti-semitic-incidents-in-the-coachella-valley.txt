SACRAMENTO –Today, Assemblyman Jeff Gonzalez (R-Indio) issued the following statement in response to the recent anti-Semitic incidents occurring throughout the Coachella Valley:

“I am deeply saddened by the recent wave of anti-Semitic incidents in the Coachella Valley. Acts of hate have no place in our communities. I stand in solidarity with the Jewish community and all those affected by these hateful acts. We must come together to reject antisemitism and all forms of hate.”
