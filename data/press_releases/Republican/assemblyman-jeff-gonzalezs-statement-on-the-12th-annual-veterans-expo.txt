INDIO – Assemblyman Jeff Gonzalez (R-Indio) issued the following statement on the 12th Annual Veterans Expo held this past Saturday in the Coachella Valley:

“This past weekend, I had the honor of attending the 12th Annual Veterans Expo. As both a State Assemblyman and a proud combat Marine veteran, I am truly grateful for the opportunity to connect with fellow veterans and access valuable resources. A special thank you to Bill and Deanna Young for their leadership and dedication in organizing this event, as well as to the countless volunteers who gave their time to make it a success. Your support is invaluable, and together, we continue to strengthen our community and provide veterans with the resources they deserve.”
