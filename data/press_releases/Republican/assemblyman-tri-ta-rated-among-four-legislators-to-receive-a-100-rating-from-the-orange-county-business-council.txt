Assemblyman Tri Ta Rated Among Four Legislators to Receive a 100% Rating from
the Orange County Business Council
The Orange County Business Council recently announced that Assemblyman Tri Ta awarded as one of six ‘Legislators of the Year’ and was one of just four legislators to receive the prestigious perfect score.
ORANGE COUNTY—The Orange County Business Council released their 2023 legislative scorecard and Assemblyman Tri Ta received a perfect score of 100%.
“I am so honored to work with the Orange County Business Council to advocate for the interests of the business community in Orange County,” said Assemblyman Tri Ta.  “The membership of the Orange County Business Council represents the economic engine of Orange County and I am pleased to receive this recognition.”
“It is my privilege to recognize and thank Assemblymember Ta for his dedicated service to Orange County”, added Jeffrey Ball, President and CEO of Orange County Business Council.  “OCBC commends his commitment to advancing economic development in our region.”
The
rating
was based on twelve votes highlighting policy priorities on housing, workforce development, employment practices, and other factors.
