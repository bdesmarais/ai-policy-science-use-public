Assemblyman Tri Ta Releases Statement Following Former Supervisor Andrew Do’s Guilty Plea
ORANGE COUNTY—Today, Assemblyman Tri Ta released the following statement following Former Supervisor Andrew Do’s guilty plea and resignation:
Public corruption cannot be tolerated. I am disappointed that Former Supervisor Andrew Do betrayed his constituents and our community.  I am thankful to the OC District Attorney, the US Attorney and all involved law enforcement in bringing this matter to justice.
This is why we need more transparency in government to prevent future betrayals of the public trust.
