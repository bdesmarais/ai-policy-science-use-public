SACRAMENTO
(March 4, 2025) – Assemblymember Diane Dixon (R-Newport Beach), along with Assemblymember Laurie Davies (R-Laguna Niguel) and Assemblymember Kate Sanchez (R-Temecula), will be hosting an event in honor of women’s history month to highlight the many tools and opportunities available to their female constituents to build financial security.
“Please join us to learn from the financial experts,” said Assemblymember Dixon. “This workshop is designed to be a hands-on, empowering opportunity to create financial success and master effective tips on financial planning.”
Emceed by the Orange County Business Council (OCBC), the Women’s Financial Freedom Conference will bring together professionals to share their insights on financial planning, credit management, real estate, investing and savings. Women can learn from groups such as the Financial Planning Association of OC, Orange County’s Credit Union, Orange County Realtors and Spinnaker Investment Group.
The event will be held in South Orange County on Friday, March 14 from 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM at Laguna Hills Community Center (25555 Alicia Parkway, Laguna Hills, CA).
