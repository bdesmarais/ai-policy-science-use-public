YUBA COUNTY – Beale Air Force Base has been selected as the
preferred site for the U.S. Air Force’s new Collaborative Combat Aircraft (CCA) program
. This next-generation drone initiative that will shape the future of air combat. The move brings national defense innovation, high-tech jobs, and economic momentum to the North State.
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (R-East Nicolaus),
who secured bi-partisan support in the Legislature for the CCA mission at Beale
, issued the following statement:
“Beale Air Force Base just landed a major mission, and it’s a game-changer for the North State. The Air Force selected Beale for its new Collaborative Combat Aircraft program, bringing next-generation autonomous aircraft and investment to our region.
This keeps Beale at the forefront of national defense and delivers high-paying jobs and advanced technology to Yuba County.
Credit to Congressman Doug LaMalfa, who fought hard in Washington to keep Beale at the top of the list. His advocacy helped deliver this major win for our community.
Beale’s future is strong — and I’ll keep fighting to make sure our region continues to lead the way in innovation, security, and opportunity.”
###
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher represents California’s 3rd Assembly District, which includes Butte, Glenn, Sutter, Tehama, Yuba, and parts of Placer County.
