SACRAMENTO – Today, in response to the arrest of two teens accused of planning a violent attack in Tehama County, Assemblyman James Gallagher (R-East Nicolaus) issued the following statement commending the swift action of the Tehama County Sheriff’s Office and local law enforcement:
“I want to thank Tehama County Sheriff Dave Kain and all involved law enforcement for their swift action in stopping a potential tragedy. Their vigilance and dedication to public safety protected countless lives. We are grateful for their service and commitment to keeping our communities safe.”
