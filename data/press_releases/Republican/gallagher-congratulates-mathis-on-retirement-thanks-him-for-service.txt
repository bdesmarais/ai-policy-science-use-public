“Devon has been a tireless advocate for his constituents and a valued member of our caucus.”
SACRAMENTO – Today,
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher
(Yuba City) issued the following statement on the announcement by
Assemblyman Devon Mathis
(R-Porterville) that he will retire from the California State Assembly at the end of his current term.
I want to thank Assemblyman Mathis for his dedicated service to the people of California and the Central Valley. Throughout his time in the Assembly, Devon has been a tireless advocate for his constituents and a valued member of our caucus. He has worked hard to bring common sense solutions to the challenges facing our state, and has been a strong voice for veterans, Californians with disabilities, farmers and more.
I am grateful for Devon’s friendship and service and wish him the best in his life after the Legislature. I know he will continue to make a positive impact on his community.
