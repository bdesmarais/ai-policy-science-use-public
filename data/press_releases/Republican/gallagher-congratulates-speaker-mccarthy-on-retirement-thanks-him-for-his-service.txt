SACRAMENTO – Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher issued the following statement on the retirement of Speaker Kevin McCarthy, thanking him for his service and wishing him well in his future endeavors:
“I am grateful to Speaker Kevin McCarthy for his years of dedicated service to our party, our state, and our nation. Speaker McCarthy has been a tireless champion for conservative values and a true advocate for California.
“Speaker McCarthy played a critical role in delivering a Republican House majority, and as Speaker he advanced our Republican principles despite a Democratic Senate and President. His work to boost California Republicans led to victories around the state, at every level of government.
“I would like to thank Speaker McCarthy for his leadership and wish him the best in whatever is next.”
