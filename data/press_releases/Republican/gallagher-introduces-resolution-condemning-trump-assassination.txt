SACRAMENTO – Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (Yuba City) introduced
HR 116
, a resolution to condemn the assassination attempt on former President Trump that occurred on July 13. President Trump was wounded in the attack and was just inches away from being killed. Three rallygoers were also shot – one, Corey Comperatore, was killed while heroically protecting his family and two others were seriously wounded.
“Violence based on someone’s political beliefs has no place in our society,
said Gallagher.
“The attempt on President Trump’s life was a wake-up call for Americans to come together and condemn political violence.”
The resolution, coauthored by the entire Assembly Republican Caucus, highlights calls for peace from both President Trump and President Biden and urges a renewed effort to foster civil discourse and encourage non-violence in our politics.
