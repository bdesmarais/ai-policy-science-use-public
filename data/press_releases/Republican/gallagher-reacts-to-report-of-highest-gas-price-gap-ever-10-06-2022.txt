Gas is Now $2.55 More Expensive in California
SACRAMENTO-
Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (Yuba City) responded to the latest
report
that Californians are currently paying over $2.55 more for a gallon of gas more than the rest of the nation-
the highest differential on record.
“Governor Newsom and Capitol Democrats are as dumb as they want to be. People have friends in other states who are paying around $3.50 a gallon. It’s laughable and Californians aren’t buying it,”
said Gallagher.
Another dismal record the state set earlier this week shows that
California has the worst maintained roads
in the nation, even though most of the gas tax is intended for road maintenance.
It has now officially been
211 days
since the Democrats promised action on soaring gas prices.
