SACRAMENTO
– Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (Yuba City) issued the following statement in response to Capitol Democrats’ latest
attempt to investigate
high gas prices:
“The price of gas has been crushing family budgets for months. If Capitol Democrats were really doing everything in their power to lower gas prices, they would support our call to suspend the gas tax and halt the scheduled July 1st increase. Californians don’t need another dead-end study, they need relief now.”
