SACRAMENTO – Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (R-Yuba City) is pleased to announce he is seeking nominations for the Assembly District 3 2022 Woman of the Year Awards to recognize the remarkable contributions made by the women of the Third Assembly District. This year he will be honoring a nominee from each of the six counties he represents (Butte, Colusa, Glenn, Sutter, Tehama, and Yuba). The nomination period will close May 6, 2022. There will be a special North State event this summer to formally recognize the honorees. Nominations can be submitted on Assemblyman Gallagher’s website at
www.assembly.ca.gov/Gallagher
.
