SACRAMENTO – Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (East Nicolaus) issued the following statement on Assemblyman Bill Essayli’s nomination to serve as United States Attorney for the Central District of California:
Bill Essayli is a strong advocate for common sense and public safety in California – I can’t think of a better person to serve as one of California’s top law enforcement officials. Through his time in the Assembly, Bill’s principled leadership and legal expertise have made him a great asset to his constituents and the Republican Caucus. I am sure he will continue to serve California well in his next chapter as U.S. Attorney.
