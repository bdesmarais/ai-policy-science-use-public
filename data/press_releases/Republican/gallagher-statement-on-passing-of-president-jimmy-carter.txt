SACRAMENTO – Today, Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher (Yuba City) issued the following statement on the passing of President Jimmy Carter:
President Carter lived a life of faith, humility, and service. His work with Habitat for Humanity, giving generously of his time and expertise, set a powerful example of what it means to serve with purpose and compassion. President Carter leaves a remarkable legacy through his unwavering devotion to his faith and to helping others. I extend my condolences to the Carter family
