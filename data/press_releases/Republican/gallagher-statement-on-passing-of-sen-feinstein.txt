SACRAMENTO – Today,
Assembly Republican Leader James Gallagher
(Yuba City) issued the following statement offering his condolences on the passing of Sen. Diane Feinstein:
Senator Feinstein led a distinguished career of public service to California. While
we didn’t always agree, she had a remarkable ability to reach across the aisle to deliver results for her constituents. That’s something we need more of. Senator Feinstein and her family are in my prayers as California grieves the loss of an icon
