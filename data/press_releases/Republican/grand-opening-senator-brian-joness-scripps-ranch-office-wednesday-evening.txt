WHO? Senator Brian Jones (R-San Diego), Local Schools, Restaurants, Non-profits, Community Groups, and Residents of Senate District 40 (North and East San Diego County, and the City of San Diego).

WHAT? A grand opening and legislative open house of Senator Jones’s new Scripps Ranch Office. The celebration, hosted by Senator Jones, is open to all residents and community groups of Senate District 40.

WHEN? Wednesday, October 4, 2023, 6:00-8:00pm

WHERE? Senator Jones’s District Office, 10650 Treena Street #110, San Diego, CA 92131

To RSVP , please visit https://tinyurl.com/JonesFallOpenHouse

For media questions, please contact Nina Krishel at Nina.Krishel@sen.ca.gov

WHY? The open house will help familiarize the community with Senator Jones’s new office. The event is an opportunity to meet Senator Jones, talk to staff, get help with casework and state agency issues, and learn about legislation and various organizations and businesses in the district.
