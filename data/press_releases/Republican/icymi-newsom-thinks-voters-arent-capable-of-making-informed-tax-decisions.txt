SACRAMENTO – This month, Governor Newsom and other Democrat politicians attempted to stop the Taxpayer Protection Act in the California Supreme Court.
In an
opinion piece
in the Sacramento Bee, Assembly Republican Leader slams Newsom’s hypocritical attempt to remove a qualified voter initiative from the ballot.
“The governor and legislative leaders — using taxpayer dollars no less — are suing to disenfranchise millions of California voters by kicking the Taxpayer Protection Act off the ballot. Why? Because this measure lets voters, not politicians, have the final say on how they’re taxed.”
With some of the highest taxes in the nation, it’s time voters have more of a say-and Democrat politicians have no business trying to stop them.
