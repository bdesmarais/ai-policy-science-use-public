SACRAMENTO
(September 10, 2024) – Assemblymember Diane Dixon (R-Newport Beach) will be hosting two local events this month for constituents to meet and interact with public safety officers.
“Please join me for an opportunity to build relationships with the officers serving our local neighborhoods over a delicious cup of coffee,” said Assemblymember Dixon.
Her two “Coffee with a Cop” events are scheduled to take place in Laguna Beach and Huntington Beach. Constituents can meet California Highway Patrol Officers and discuss community issues such as E-Bike safety, the Pacific Coast Highway, parking and public safety.
The first Coffee with a Cop event will be held in Huntington Beach on Monday, September 16 from 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM at 1 Oz Coffee (155 5th St. Ste 121, Huntington Beach, CA).
The following event will be held in Laguna Beach on Wednesday, September 25, from 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM at Jedidiah Coffee (397 North Coast Highway, Laguna Beach, CA).
