State Senator Brian Jones (R-Santee) today released the latest edition of his acclaimed “Are You Kidding Me?” (AYKM) video series questioning government actions and decisions that just don’t make any sense.

Today’s AYKM has to do with the California High Speed Rail Authority using phony job numbers to justify its existence.
