SACRAMENTO
– Tomorrow, ahead of the first committee hearing of the special session on Gov. Gavin Newsom’s refinery mandate, Assembly Republicans will hold a press conference discussing their proposals which will actually lower gas prices and offer Californians immediate relief at the pump.
Newsom called the special session after not getting his way on a rushed, last-minute proposal to add storage regulations on refineries. Even his own administration has warned this
could cause fuel shortages and increase gas prices
. It’s time for real solutions.
Republican proposals to bring down gas prices include:
Exempt Gas from Cap-and-Trade (ABX2-3 – Gallagher)
Suspend the Gas Tax (ABX2-2 – Lackey)
Increase Transparency on Gasoline Prices (ABX2-7 – Dixon)
Stop Newsom’s Stealth Gas Tax Increase (ABX2-4 – Joe Patterson)
$100 Rebate for Drivers to Offset Rising Fuel Costs (ABX2-5 – Lackey)
Cut Red Tape to Expand California’s Storage Projects (ABX2-6 – Lackey)
