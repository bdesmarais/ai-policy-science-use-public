Senator Tony Strickland ( R-Huntington Beach ) is excited to invite friends, constituents, community leaders, and elected officials to tour his new district office in Huntington Beach.

In line with his commitment to the 36th Senate District, this office will serve as a vital resource for the community, acting as the front door to state government. The grand opening and community open house will provide a valuable opportunity for residents to meet Senator Strickland and his district staff, who will assist with casework, legislative inquiries, and community outreach.

WHO :      Senator Tony Strickland and staff

WHAT :    Senate District 36 Grand Opening and Community Open House

WHEN :   Thursday, May 29, 2025 from 4:30PM-7:00PM

WHERE:

Senator Strickland’s District Office

301 Main Street, Suite 212

Huntington Beach, CA 92648

For questions to attend the grand opening celebration or media inquiries about other topics/issues, please contact Jacqui.Nguyen@sen.ca.gov by email or call/text at (858) 999-7706.
