Senator Shannon Grove ( R-Bakersfield ) along with supporters of Senate Bill 64 will hold a rally prior to the bill presentation in the California State Senate Education Committee near the West Steps of the California State Capitol. SB 64, the School Choice Flex Account Act , would transform K-12 education in California by giving parents the freedom to choose the best-accredited school for their child, with funding following the student.

After the rally, Senator Grove and supporters will head to the California Senate Education Committee where she will present SB 64 to the committee members for a vote.
