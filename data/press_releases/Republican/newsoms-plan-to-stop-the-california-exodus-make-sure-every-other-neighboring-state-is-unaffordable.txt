SACRAMENTO
– Governor Newsom is doing his part to stop the California exodus: by making sure every other surrounding state is just as expensive.
This weekend he was in Washington State promoting their new Cap-and-Trade program, which has
driven up their gas prices by 50 cents/gallon
. This is just his latest attempt to ensure gas prices remain sky high for everyone in the West.
A week ago, Democrats in California jammed Newsom’s
expensive refinery mandate
through the Legislature – a bill that even the
Governors of Arizona and Nevada
warned would drive up costs in their states.
If that wasn’t bad enough, his administration is gearing up to take a vote on new regulations to the Low Carbon Fuel Standard, a decision that could push California gas prices another 65 cents per gallon. Representatives from his administration have
refused to provide an updated cost estimate
to lawmakers and journalists.
Wouldn’t it be great if Newsom would fix problems in our state instead of causing them in others?
