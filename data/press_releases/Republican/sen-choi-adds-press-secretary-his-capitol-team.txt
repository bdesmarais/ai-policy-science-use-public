As he finishes building his Capitol office staff, Senator Steven Choi (R- Irvine) has brought on Kelley Rooney to fill the position of Press Secretary. Rooney hails from Fair Oaks and brings a wealth of experience in communications and advocacy to the team.

“I am pleased to have Kelley on our team and look forward to expanding our social and traditional media presence,” said Senator Choi. “Completing my Capitol staff will allow me to best serve my constituents in District 37.”

In addition to Rooney, Senator Choi announces his additional Capitol team members, including:

David Orosco , Chief of Staff

Spencer Rhoads , Legislative Director

Marissa Dunn , Scheduler

Karan Brar , Legislative Aide

Meri Kaftan , Policy Analyst

Madeline Lowe , Legislative Intern

In his district office the team includes:

Kyle Swarts , District Director

Savannah Watson , District Representative

Yun Choi , District Representative

Lillian Grabowski , District Representative

Justin Graves , District Representative
