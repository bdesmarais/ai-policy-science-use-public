As part of her ongoing commitment to serve her constituents, Senate Republican Leader Shannon Grove ( R-Bakersfield ) will host mobile office hours for the community of Trona on Tuesday, August 13th.

Constituents of California's 16th Senate District are invited to meet with staff and learn about state issues and resources to help them rebuild following the earthquakes, as well as other services that are available through Senator Grove’s office.

Office of Senate Republican Leader Shannon Grove
