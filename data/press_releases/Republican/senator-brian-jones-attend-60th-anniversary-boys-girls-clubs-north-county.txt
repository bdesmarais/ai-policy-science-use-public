Who?

Senator Brian Jones (R-Santee), Boys & Girls Clubs leaders, staff and members, and local community leaders and residents.

What?

A ribbon-cutting and the 60 th Anniversary celebration of the Boys & Girls Clubs of North County.

When?

Thursday, July 7, 2022, 4:00pm.

Where?

Ingold Clubhouse – Boys & Girls Clubs of North County, 445 East Ivy Street, Fallbrook, CA 92028. Please RSVP to Brian Astredo at brianpa@bgcnorthcounty.org or (760) 728-5871

Why?

To celebrate and show support for the Boys & Girls Clubs of North County.
