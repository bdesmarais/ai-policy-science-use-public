Who?

Senator Brian Jones (R-Santee)

What?

A tele-town hall for a legislative update from Senator Jones on what is happening in Sacramento, in District 38, and with the COVID-19 pandemic.

Where?

Your phone. (Call-in number will be provided to those who RSVP).

When?

6:00pm on Wednesday, August 5, 2020

Why?

Senator Jones will be discussing what he has been doing to assist constituents during the COVID-19 pandemic. Additionally, as the legislative session approaches an end in Sacramento, Senator Jones will be providing updates about his legislation and his thoughts on the Legislature this year.

Nothing is more important to the Senator than hearing from his constituents. Senator Jones will be answering your questions during the event. Because of the limitation of teleconference, we are asking for all questions to be submitted in advance. The deadline for question submissions and RSVPs is Tuesday August 4th at noon.

How?

RSVP and submit your questions: https://bit.ly/JonesTeleTownHallAugust2020
