Who?

Senator Brian Jones ( R-San Diego ) and staff, Local Schools, Restaurants, Non-profits, Community Groups, and Residents of Senate District 40 (North and East San Diego County, and the City of San Diego).

What?

Annual legislative open house at Senator Jones’s Scripps Ranch Office. The celebration, hosted by Senator Jones, is open to all residents and community groups of Senate District 40. Refreshments will be provided.

Several community groups will have booths at the open house, including:

University City Community Association (UCCA) Poway Chamber of Commerce National Black Contractors Association of USA Inc Wounded Warrior Homes San Dieguito River Park JPA/Sikes Adobe Historic Farmstead UC San Diego Alliant International University Home of Guiding Hands Pacific Southwest Association of Realtors East County Transitional Living Center Feeding San Diego California State University, San Marcos International Facility Management Association of San Diego Scripps Ranch Civic Association Deeper Still Fallbrook D'Vine Path Fallbrook Village Rotary Mira Mesa Town Council El Cajon Valley Host Lions Club National University Neighborhood Healthcare

Where?

Senator Jones’s District Office

10650 Treena Street #110

San Diego, CA 92131

When?

Saturday, December 14, 2024

12pm-3pm

Why?

The annual open house brings the community together at Senator Jones’s office. The event is an opportunity to meet Senator Jones, talk to staff, get help with casework and state agency issues, and learn about legislation and various organizations and businesses in the district. Senator Jones will also provide an update about the district and his important work in Sacramento.
