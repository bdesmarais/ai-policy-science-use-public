Who?

Senator Brian W. Jones (R-Santee), San Diego County District Attorney Summer Stephan, Chula Vista Police Chief Roxana Kennedy and the owner and employees of Barrio Auto Service.

What?

An in-person, outdoor press conference discussing Jones’s Senate Bill 919 , dealing with catalytic converter thefts and a demonstration by the employees of Barrio Auto Service on how easily and quickly a catalytic converter can be stolen from a vehicle.

Where?

Barrio Auto Service , 2490 Commercial Street, San Diego, CA 92113

For more information please contact John Mendez at (619) 607-0007 or john.mendez@sen.ca.gov .

When?

Friday, March 18, 2022, 10:30am.

Why?

For credentialed media to get an update on Senate Bill 919 and to see firsthand how a catalytic converter can be stolen from a vehicle in a matter of a few minutes.
