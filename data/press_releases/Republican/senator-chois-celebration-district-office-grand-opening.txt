Friday evening, Senator Steven Choi (R- Irvine) welcomed constituents, local leaders, and community members to his new Irvine District Office for a lively open house event that marked the formal opening of the new District Office.

Over the course of the evening, guests toured the new office space, enjoyed light food and beverages, and had the opportunity to speak directly with Senator Choi and his district staff. The event ran from 5:00pm to 7:00pm and included a brief welcome from the Senator.

Attendees included city council members, school board trustees, deacons from local churches, and dozens of constituents from across the 37 th Senate District. Invitations were also extended to elected officials in surrounding districts.

“It was a pleasure to welcome so many familiar faces and new ones as we officially open our doors to the community,” said Senator Choi. “This office is here to serve the people of the 37 th District, and I’m proud to have a space where constituents can connect with their state government in a more personal, accessible way.”

The new District Office is now open for regular business Monday through Friday, 9:00am to 5:00pm. Residents are encouraged to reach out with questions, concerns, or to schedule meetings with staff.
