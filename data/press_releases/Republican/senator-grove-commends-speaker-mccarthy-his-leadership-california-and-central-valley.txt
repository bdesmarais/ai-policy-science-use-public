California State Senator Shannon Grove (R-Bakersfield) issued the following statement regarding the announcement by Speaker Kevin McCarthy (R-CA) that he will be leaving Congress at the end of the year.

“Speaker Kevin McCarthy has been a tremendous advocate for the Central Valley, the State of California, and our country. He is a patriot, a loyal Republican, and most of all a dear friend.

“While Congress will miss his experience and talent, I am excited that he will continue to fight for not just Republicans, but for the Central Valley as well.

“I am thankful for Kevin’s service to the country and look forward to continue working side-by-side with him in the future.”
