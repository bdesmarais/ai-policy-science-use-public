Senator Shannon Grove (R-Bakersfield) will celebrate the California State Senate District 16 2022 Small Business of the Year, Huddleston Crane Service at a ceremony in Taft on Wednesday, October 26, 2022.

Each year, California legislators have the opportunity to nominate a small business in their respective districts. The Small Business of the Year award celebrates extraordinary small businesses, their owners and employees throughout the state. Honorees are presented with a framed resolution from the Legislature to recognize their outstanding achievements.
