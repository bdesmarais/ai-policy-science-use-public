El Cajon – On its recent “2019 Legislative Report Card,” the Howard Jarvis Taxpayers Association (HJTA) announced that Senator Brian Jones (R-Santee) received an “A” grade and scored 100%, concurring with HJTA’s position on nine major measures dealing with taxes and other fiscal issues important to taxpayers.

“The taxpayers in North and East San Diego County are very well represented by Senator Brian Jones,” said Jon Coupal, President of the Howard Jarvis Taxpayers Association.  “Brian Jones is a strong tax-fighter and an effective leader for hard-working families.”

Jones’ career vote ratings by the HJTA:

2019:  100% “A” 2016:    97% “A” 2015:  100% “A” 2014:    97% “A” 2013:  100% “A” 2012:    98% “A” 2011:    94% “A”
