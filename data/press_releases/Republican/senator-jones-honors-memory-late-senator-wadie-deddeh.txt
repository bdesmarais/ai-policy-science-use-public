State Capitol – Senator Brian Jones (R-Santee) today joined other Senators in honoring the memory of the late Senator Wadie Deddeh who faithfully and effectively served the San Diego community as a member of the State Assembly and State Senate for 27 years, from 1967 to 1993.

Although Deddeh was a well-respected Democrat, that did not prevent him from graciously mentoring Jones and other Republican elected officials.

The Senate adjourned today in memory of Senator Deddeh.
