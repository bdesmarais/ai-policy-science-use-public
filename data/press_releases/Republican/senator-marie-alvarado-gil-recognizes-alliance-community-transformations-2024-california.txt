Senator Alvarado-Gil (D-Jackson) awarded The Alliance for Community Transformations for their exceptional commitment to providing responsive and preventative services to individuals and families and its dedication to fostering safe, healthy, and socially just communities where every member thrives.

The Alliance for Community Transformations, serving Mariposa and Merced Counties, operates six vital programs that contribute to its mission: Mountain Crisis Services, Valley Crisis Center, Ethos Youth Center, Mariposa Heritage House, CASA (Court-Appointed Special Advocates) of Mariposa, and Connections emergency shelter. Since its founding in 1990 as Mountain Crisis Services, the organization has grown and evolved, reflecting its expanded scope and vision.

Senator Alvarado-Gil commended the Alliance for its impactful work, stating, "The Alliance exemplifies the spirit of service and dedication that makes our communities stronger and more resilient. Their approach to addressing critical issues including domestic violence, youth empowerment, and homelessness sets a standard for excellence. I proudly recognize their outstanding contributions and commitment to making a difference in the lives of so many."

For more information about the Alliance for Community Transformations and its programs, please visit www.alliance4community.org .
