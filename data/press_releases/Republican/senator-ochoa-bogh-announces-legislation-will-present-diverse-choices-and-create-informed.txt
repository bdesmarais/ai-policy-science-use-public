Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh >( R-Yucaipa ) will hold a press conference on Wednesday, April 24 to discuss her legislation, Senate Bill 1368 prior to presenting the measure in the Senate Education Committee. SB 1368 would update the comprehensive sexual health education for students to include information about licensed pregnancy centers as part of the instruction on local resources for sexual health, pregnancy and beyond. The bill would not replace current curriculum or existing resources.

Upon conclusion of the press conference, Senator Ochoa Bogh and advocates will head to the Senate Education Committee, where she will present the measure to the committee.
