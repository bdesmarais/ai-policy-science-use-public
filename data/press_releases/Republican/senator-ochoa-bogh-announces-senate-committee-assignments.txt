Sacramento – Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh (R-Yucaipa) announced today her committee assignments for the 2021-22 legislative session:

Banking & Financial Institutions (Vice Chair)

Budget & Fiscal Review

Business, Professions & Economic Development

Education (Vice Chair)

Housing

Labor, Public Employment & Retirement (Vice Chair)

Public Safety (Vice Chair)

“I am excited to be able to serve on committees that address many of the issues impacting our state and that reflect the priorities of my constituents such as the Economy, Public Safety, Education and Housing,” said Senator Ochoa Bogh. “I look forward to working collaboratively with my colleagues to ensure a fair political balance is achieved while working diligently in the best interest of all Californians.”
