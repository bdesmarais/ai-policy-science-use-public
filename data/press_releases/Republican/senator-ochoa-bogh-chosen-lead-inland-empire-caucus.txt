Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh (R-Yucaipa) announced that she has been named Chair of the Inland Empire Caucus.

“I am honored my colleagues have elected me to serve as the Chair of the Inland Empire Caucus,” said Senator Ochoa Bogh. “The Inland Empire is a vibrant region with unique opportunities and challenges. I look forward to working with my fellow legislators to amplify the voice of our region and provide our communities the proper tools to succeed and thrive.”

The Inland Empire Caucus is a bi-partisan group of legislators who represent San Bernardino and Riverside Counties. The caucus works together to address and advocate for solutions to local issues impacting the region, with the ultimate goal of improving the quality of life for local residents.
