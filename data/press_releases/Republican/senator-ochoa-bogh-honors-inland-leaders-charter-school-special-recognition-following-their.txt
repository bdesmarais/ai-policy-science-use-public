Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh (R-Yucaipa) presented the Inland Leaders Charter School with a Senate Resolution celebrating their recent achievement after receiving the 2023 CA Distinguished School Award for their exemplary and quality educational programs.

"It was with great pride and honor that I presented the Inland Leaders Charter School with a Senate Resolution," said Senator Ochoa Bogh. "A quality education can make all the difference in a child's life. Inland Leaders Charter School provides an innovative learning environment where students acquire the skills needed to succeed. Congratulations on being named a 2023 CA Distinguished School."

The Inland Leaders Charter School is a kindergarten (including TK) through 8th-grade public charter school dedicated to creating leaders for the 21st century. Their mission is to provide education for all students that will equip them with the critical skills necessary to be successful leaders through innovative practices, accelerative achievement, and cultivating strong leadership.

“Inland Leaders is proud and excited to be honored as a California Distinguished School by Senator Ochoa Bogh,” said Michael Gordon, Executive Director of Inland Leaders Charter School. “Our staff, students, and parents have worked extremely hard to perform at the highest academic levels for many years. We look forward to the program's continued success in our mission to train leaders for our communities.”

As part of The California Department of Education’s (CDE) School Recognition Program , the California Distinguished Schools Award is one of the many ways CDE celebrates excellence in education and honors exemplary students, teachers, classified employees, and schools.
