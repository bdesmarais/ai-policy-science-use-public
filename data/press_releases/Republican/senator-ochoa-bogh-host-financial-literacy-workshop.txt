Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh (R-Yucaipa) will host a Financial Literacy Workshop to help raise awareness of the importance of financial literacy and maintaining smart money management habits.

“Education is power. I am excited to facilitate an opportunity for my constituents to learn how to make sound and effective financial decisions,” said Senator Ochoa Bogh. “Providing individuals with the tools necessary to live a stable financial life for themselves and their families reaffirms my belief that opportunity exists for those willing to learn and work for it.”
