Senator Ochoa Bogh engaging her constituents at her town hall.

Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh ( R-Yucaipa ) is pleased to announce that she successfully hosted her 50th community town hall! This marks a significant milestone in her ongoing commitment to connecting with constituents across the Inland Empire. The event, which took place in Yucaipa, was attended by a diverse group of residents from the area. They engaged in meaningful discussions about the issues that matter most to them and their community.

“I am deeply grateful to everyone who has attended my town halls, shared their perspectives, and brought critical issues to the forefront of our conversations,” said Senator Ochoa Bogh . “Your voices are essential in shaping the work we do in Sacramento, and I am honored to represent such an engaged and passionate community.”

The town hall series, a cornerstone of Senator Ochoa Bogh’s approach to representation, has provided a vital platform for open dialogue, ensuring that the voices of the Inland Empire are heard loud and clear in Sacramento. These gatherings have fostered a spirit of collaboration and transparency, allowing Senator Ochoa Bogh to address concerns, answer questions, and bring constituent-driven solutions to the forefront of legislative action.

Senator Ochoa Bogh remains committed to advocating for the needs and priorities of her constituents in the Inland Empire. As their representative in Sacramento, she is dedicated to ensuring that the region’s unique challenges and opportunities are addressed through thoughtful, responsive legislation.

Senator Ochoa Bogh emphasized the importance of community involvement, stating , “The Inland Empire is one of the fastest-growing regions in the country. It’s crucial for my constituents to realize that by working together and engaging with their representatives, they can influence policy. There is strength in numbers.”

Senate District 23 residents are encouraged to stay connected and informed about the latest updates and events by signing up to receive our email e-alerts , where they will receive regular updates about town hall meetings and other important community events.

Additionally, following Senator Ochoa Bogh’s social media pages will provide constituents with real-time information, engaging content, and reminders about upcoming events and anything happening in our district.
