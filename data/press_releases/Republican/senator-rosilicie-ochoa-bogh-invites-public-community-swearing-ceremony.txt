Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh ( R-Yucaipa ) invites members of the press and the public to attend her Community Swearing-In Ceremony on Friday, March 7, from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM at the Indian Wells Theatre on the CSUSB Palm Desert Campus.

This special event will bring together community stakeholders, family, friends, and residents of Senate District 19 to witness the administration of the oath of office, marking the beginning of a new chapter in Senator Ochoa Bogh’s commitment to public service.

What: Community Swearing-In Ceremony for Senator Rosilicie Ochoa Bogh

When: Friday, March 7, 2025 | 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM

Where: Indian Wells Theatre, CSUSB Palm Desert Campus

37500 Cook Street, Palm Desert, CA 92211

The public can RSVP here , and members of the press can RSVP to Ivette.burch@sen.ca.go v .

For more Information, please contact the District Office at (909) 335-0271.
