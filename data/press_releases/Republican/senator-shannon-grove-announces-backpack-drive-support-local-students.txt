Senator Shannon Grove is pleased to announce a community backpack drive to benefit local K-12 students. The drive will take place throughout the month of July with three drop off locations in Kern County.

The backpack drive runs now through July 31 st , Monday through Friday, from 9:00am – 5:00 pm. Donations of new backpacks can be dropped off at:

Senator Shannon Grove’s Bakersfield District Office

5701 Truxtun Avenue, Suite 150

Bakersfield, CA 93309

West Side Recreation & Park District

500 Cascade Place, Building D

Taft, CA 93268

Ridgecrest City Hall

100 W. California Avenue

Ridgecrest, CA 93355

All donated backpacks will be distributed to students through the Community Action Partnership of Kern, West Side Recreation & Park District, and Church Without Walls. These organizations, along with many others, play a crucial role in supporting the educational needs of our local community.

“Every child deserves to walk into the classroom feeling confident and ready to learn,” said Senator Shannon Grove. “Even one backpack can make a difference. I’m grateful for our community’s continued generosity and support of our students.”

For more information, please contact Senator Shannon Grove’s office at (661) 323-0443.
