Today, Sen. Marie Alvarado-Gil ( R-Jackson ) released a blunt bilingual video message to address the ongoing violence in Los Angeles and set the record straight on the real cause driving the confusion around immigration enforcement.

Speaking directly to immigrant communities, Senator Alvarado-Gil acknowledges the fear and anxiety many are facing and expresses her unwavering support for families who came here to seek opportunity, work, and create a better life for their families.

In the video, she explains how California’s sanctuary policies have created a recipe for disaster, explains how Governor Newsom and progressive Sacramento Democrats have prioritized politics over public safety, and calls for an end to the violence tearing communities apart.
